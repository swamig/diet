# Batch Prep & Protocol Updates Log

**Date:** 2026-03-07

## 1. Batch Prep Execution
*   **Legumes:** Prepped Garbanzo Beans (2 cups dry) and Black Beans (2 cups dry) separately.
    *   *Rationale:* Separating them allows for different cooking velocities (garbanzos take longer) and distinct flavor rotations later in the week. Black beans can carry cinnamon/clove notes, while garbanzos pair well with the daily turmeric/cumin stack.
*   **Quinoa:** Prepped 3-4 cups dry. 
    *   *Rationale:* Thoroughly rinsed for 60 seconds to remove bitter saponins (no soaking required). Boiled for 15 minutes, rested for 5.
*   **The "Cook-and-Chill" Method:** Both the beans and quinoa were cooled rapidly on a tray and placed in the fridge for 24 hours before consumption.
    *   *Rationale:* This cooling phase (retrogradation) converts the simple starches into **Resistant Starch (Type 3)**, which lowers the glycemic index and acts as a powerful prebiotic for the microbiome.

## 2. Menu Adjustments
*   **Green Beans:** Excluded from the daily broccoli block, as they do not provide the necessary glucoraphanin for Sulforaphane activation. 
    *   *Action:* Lacto-fermenting them in a 2% salt brine (with garlic and cumin) for 5-7 days.
    *   *Rationale:* This converts an off-protocol vegetable into a highly beneficial fermented food, satisfying the daily 2 tbsp ferment requirement without disrupting the primary fiber strategy.

## 3. Supplement Stack Integration ("Finish Your Stash" Protocol)
*   **Chocolate/Vanilla Flavored Whey (2 scoops):** Moved strictly to the 17:30 post-workout window.
    *   *Rationale:* To mitigate the oxalate load from chocolate whey, raw cacao nibs must be skipped in the evening Recovery Bowl on these days. Artificial sweeteners in flavored whey can disrupt microbiome hormesis, so consumption is strictly timed to the post-workout window. Target state is Unflavored Whey.
*   **Creatine (5g):** Taken daily at 17:30 with the whey.
    *   *Rationale:* The insulin response from the protein and the subsequent main meal maximizes creatine uptake into muscle cells.
*   **Mud Water (1 tbsp):** Cycled 1-2x a week on non-cacao/non-turmeric days.
    *   *Rationale:* Prevents systemic oxalate and curcumin overload, protecting liver and kidney pathways.
*   **Moringa Powder (1 tbsp):** Cycled 2x a week (e.g., Tues/Thurs).
    *   *Rationale:* Provides a mild hormetic stress without overwhelming liver detox pathways. Not to be stacked with heavy antimicrobial spices (Clove/Ajwain).
