# Logs

[Back to Manual](../README.md)

The logs convert the manual from theory into evidence. Use them to capture what was actually eaten, how prep changed, what substitutions were made, and what the body did in response.

## Log Areas

- [Eating Logs](eating/README.md)
- [Eating Log Template](eating/template.md)
- [Cooking & Prep Logs](cooking/README.md)
- [Cooking Log Template](cooking/template.md)

## Naming Convention

- Use `YYYY-MM-DD-description.md` for new log files.
- Keep one entry per file when possible so logs stay searchable and linkable.

## Minimum Useful Logging

- One eating log per day during active execution.
- One cooking log per prep session or whenever a recipe changes materially.
- Explicit notes when protocol rules are missed, substituted, or intentionally modified.

## Review Loop

1. Capture what actually happened in an eating or cooking log.
2. Score the week lightly in the [Symptom & Training Scorecard](../06-monitoring/symptom-training-scorecard.md).
3. Convert those notes into a decision in the [Weekly Review Template](../06-monitoring/weekly-review.md).
