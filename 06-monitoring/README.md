[Back to Manual](../README.md)

This section turns the protocol into a feedback loop. Use it to decide whether the system is actually improving inflammation, performance, recovery, and adherence.

## Minimum Useful Cadence

- Before Day 1: capture baseline markers, body weight, and training status.
- Weekly: review energy, digestion, training quality, and adherence notes.
- End of Week 4: compare outcomes, then decide what should be repeated, reduced, or removed.

## What This Section Now Covers

- Starting-state capture before the cycle.
- Lightweight daily or weekly symptom and training scoring.
- Weekly decision reviews during the cycle.
- End-of-cycle comparison and retention decisions.
