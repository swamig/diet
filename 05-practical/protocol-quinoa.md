# The Protocol Quinoa Rice (RS3 Anchor)

Quinoa is utilized in this protocol not as a standard grain, but as a fiber-dense delivery vehicle for **Resistant Starch Type 3 (RS3)**. When prepared using the Double-Chill method, it provides a slow-release glucose signal that sustains afternoon physical performance without an insulin spike [[Birt, 2013]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#7-gut-microbiome--resistant-starch-type-3).

## Ingredients (Weekly Batch)

*   **2 cups Dry Quinoa** (Tri-color or white).
*   **4 cups Water** (Elite Hack: Use reserved chickpea **Aquafaba** or Bone Broth for umami depth).
*   **The Lycopene Sofrito Base:** 1 cup pureed tomatoes OR **3 tbsp Tomato Paste** thinned with water (Paste is the elite, hyper-concentrated source of cis-lycopene [[Patra, 2020]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#10-antioxidants-polyphenols--vascular-signaling)) + 1/2 white onion (finely diced) + 2 cloves garlic (minced and rested 10 min) [[Song & Milner, 2001]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#3-allicin-activation-the-10-minute-garlic-rule).
*   **The Veggie Mix:** 1/2 cup green peas (frozen or fresh) + 1/2 cup finely diced carrots.
*   **The Umami Signal:** 1 tsp **Soy Sauce (Tamari)** or 1/2 tsp **Nutritional Yeast** (provides the savory bouillon profile).
*   **The Tocotrienol Booster:** 1 tsp **Achiote (Annatto) powder** + 1 tsp whole Cumin seeds.
*   **The Floral Heat:** 1 whole Serrano or Jalapeño chili (Do NOT chop it. Leave it whole).
*   **1 tsp Sea Salt** & **1 tbsp Avocado Oil.**

## The Double-Chill Protocol (Mandatory)

1.  **The Rinse:** Rinse dry quinoa in a fine-mesh strainer for 60 seconds to remove saponins.
2.  **The Sofrito Toast:** Heat 1 tbsp of Avocado Oil in a large pot on medium heat. Add the diced onion and rested garlic. Sauté for 2 minutes until translucent. Add the rinsed quinoa and cumin seeds. Toast for 3 minutes, stirring frequently, until the quinoa smells nutty.
3.  **The Lycopene Reduction:** Pour in the pureed tomatoes and Achiote powder. Stir continuously for 2 minutes until the tomato reduces slightly and deepens in color (this maximizes the bioavailability of the cis-lycopene) [[Patra, 2020]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#10-antioxidants-polyphenols--vascular-signaling).
4.  **The Boil:** Add the liquid (Water/Broth/Aquafaba), salt, carrots, peas, and the **Umami Signal** (Tamari/Nut Yeast). Drop in the **whole Serrano chili**. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-18 minutes until the liquid is absorbed. 
5.  **The "Restaurant" Finish:** Remove from heat. Remove and discard the whole chili. Immediately grate the **Zest of 1 Lime** and add the juice of half a lime directly into the pot. Fold in a handful of finely chopped cilantro stems.
6.  **The First Chill:** Spread the hot "Mexican" quinoa onto a flat stainless steel baking sheet. Place in the fridge immediately [[Mohan, 2015]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#7-gut-microbiome--resistant-starch-type-3).
7.  **The 24h Lock:** Store the cooled quinoa in a large glass container for **at least 24 hours** before the first serving [[PMC10987757]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#7-gut-microbiome--resistant-starch-type-3).

## Execution (The 12:00 Meal)

*   **Portion Size:** 
    *   *Baseline:* 1/2 cup (cooked) per day.
    *   *VO2 Max 4x4 Days:* 3/4 cup (cooked) to replenish glycogen [[Helgerud, 2007]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#9-metabolic-flexibility-vo2-max--hiit).
    *   **The Aambali Starch Split:** If you are consuming [The Protocol Aambali](protocol-aambali.md) at 17:50, you **MUST reduce this 12:00 Quinoa portion by 50%** (e.g., to 1/4 cup) to maintain daily glycemic and caloric targets.
*   **The 130°F Thermal Ceiling:** To preserve the RS3 structure, never reheat quinoa in a microwave or high-heat pan. Reheat gently in a pan on low heat until just warm to the touch (approx. 130°F) [[Birt, 2013]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#7-gut-microbiome--resistant-starch-type-3).
*   **The Fat Bloom:** Before serving, fold in a squeeze of fresh lime juice and a drizzle of Blueprint EVOO or Avocado Oil. Achiote is fat-soluble; the oil ensures maximum absorption of the tocotrienols [[Beauchamp, 2005]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#10-antioxidants-polyphenols--vascular-signaling).
