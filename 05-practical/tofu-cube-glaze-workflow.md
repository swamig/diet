# Tofu Cube Glaze — Quick Weeknight Workflow

The simplest version of the sesame-tofu meal. Pulls a frozen tofu slab from the [Sheet 07 stash](./protocol-tofu.md), **dry-brines** it (no boil, no 5-min press), crisps it, melts a [sesame sauce cube](./sesame-sauce-cubes.md) into a glaze, and plates with finishers. Target: **~15 min day-of** after the tofu has thawed.

This is the answer to *"how do I take a cube, salt the tofu, and add the sauce — fast, after work."*

---

## 1. When to Use This vs. Full Sheet 07

| Mode | Day-of time | Use when |
|---|---|---|
| **Quick (this doc)** | ~15 min after thaw | Weeknight · low energy · single-serving |
| Full [Sheet 07](./protocol-tofu.md) (wet brine + press) | ~25 min after thaw | Batch prep · multi-serving · max texture |

The quick version trades ~10 minutes and a slightly less spongy texture for a much simpler workflow. Tofu is still excellent — the freeze-thaw upgrade did most of the structural work upstream.

---

## 2. The Dry-Brine Technique

Sprinkle kosher salt directly on the thawed slab → capillary action draws interior moisture to the surface → wipe it off → the tofu is now **seasoned from the inside** AND **surface-dried**. No brine bowl. No press under a heavy pan.

| Variable | Value |
|---|---|
| Salt | **½ tsp kosher per 200 g slab** (~¼ tsp per 100 g half-slab) |
| Rest time | **10–15 min** — moisture beading visible by minute 10 |
| Wipe | Pat with a fresh paper towel — surface should feel **slightly tacky, not wet** |

**Why it works:** osmosis at the surface pulls interior water out faster than full submersion brine; the salt that stays in the tofu after the wipe ≈ what you'd absorb from a 1 tsp/quart wet brine. Same seasoning, half the steps.

**Do not use table salt or fine sea salt** — they over-penetrate in 10 min and you'll end up with too-salty tofu. Kosher flake or coarse sea salt only.

---

## 3. Day-of Timeline (~15 min after thaw)

Times are minutes elapsed from start. The dry-brine rest is the longest single block; everything else is hands-on.

| Elapsed | Action | Why / Rule |
|---|---|---|
| 00:00 | Tofu **thawed** — fridge overnight (8–12 h) OR cold-water quick thaw (30 min). Squeeze out any free water. | Extra-firm only · no gums |
| 00:00 | Pull from freezer: **1 sesame sauce cube** OR **1 [combo cube](./veg-oyster-sriracha-combo-cubes.md)** · **1 [rested garlic cube](./freeze-system.md)** | Sheet 21 / 25 / 12 |
| 00:00 | Sprinkle **½ tsp kosher salt** evenly over both sides of the slab. Lay flat on paper towel. | Dry-brine — capillary draw |
| **00:10** | **Wipe wet salt off** with a fresh paper towel — slab is now seasoned and dried | Tactile check: tacky, not wet |
| 00:10 | Crush 2 garlic cloves · rest 10 min *(skip if using a rested-garlic cube)* | Allicin window non-negotiable |
| 00:11 | Cube tofu into **1-inch pieces** if not already | Even cooking |
| 00:11 | **Air fryer 400 °F · 15 min** OR pan-sear 60–90 sec/side in 2 tbsp avocado oil | Crispy edges target |
| 00:25 | Small pan on **low** — drop sauce cube + **2 tbsp water** · stir to melt (~60 sec) | Cube becomes glaze |
| 00:26 | Drop **rested garlic cube** · stir 30 sec **off heat** | Off heat preserves allicin |
| 00:27 | Toss crispy tofu in glaze · coat fully | Glossy / sticky / coated · not soupy |
| 00:28 | **OFF HEAT.** Drizzle **1 tsp toasted sesame oil** over tofu | Sesame oil never sees flame |
| 00:28 | Top with **1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds** + **5–6 torn mint leaves** | TRPV1 / TRPM8 synergy |
| 00:30 | **Serve immediately** | Mint volatiles dissipate fast |

The air fryer block (00:11 → 00:25) is dead time — set the timer and do the dishes from yesterday.

## 4. Cube Count by Application

| Application | Cubes | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Half block (~200 g · one meal)** | 1 cube | + 2 tbsp water + 1 tsp sesame oil |
| Full block (~400 g · two meals) | 2 cubes | Standard portion |
| Saucy noodles / stir fry | 3 cubes | + 3 tbsp water · ramp sesame oil to 1½ tsp |

If using a [combo cube](./veg-oyster-sriracha-combo-cubes.md) (sesame + oyster + sriracha pre-stacked), one cube ≈ one cube — the math doesn't change.

## 5. Optional Bio-Signal Upgrades (day-of)

All optional. Stack as desired — none of them break the timeline.

- **[Rested garlic cube](./freeze-system.md) at the pan** — strongly recommended for the allicin pulse. Already in the table above; calling out separately because skipping it is the #1 way people downgrade this meal.
- **Fresh ginger, grated** — ½ tsp into the glaze at the cube-melt step
- **Lime wedge** squeezed over plated tofu — iron + Vit C synergy with the side legume
- **Microgreens / sprouts** on top
- **Scallion, thin-sliced** — allicin top note
- **Nori strips** — iodine + free glutamate, stacks with the umami in the cube

## 7. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Tofu is rubbery / dense | Dry-brine rest too short, OR sear too hot too long | Hold the brine the full 10–15 min; air fry at **400 °F max 15 min** |
| Glaze is too thin | Cube under-reduced OR too much water added | 1 cube + 1 tbsp water · reduce on low until it coats a spoon |
| Glaze is too salty | Stacked tamari / oyster / black salt + cube too hot | **Halve the dry-brine salt sprinkle** when pairing with a salt-heavy cube (combo cube, miso glaze) |
| Sesame oil tastes burnt | You heated the sesame oil | Sesame oil **always off-heat**, final 10 sec |
| Tofu falls apart | Wrong tofu (silken / soft) OR pressed too hard | Extra-firm only · pat dry, don't crush |
| Tofu won't crisp | Surface still wet after wipe OR overcrowded air fryer | Single layer in the basket · re-wipe if visibly damp |

---

## 8. Critical Rules (recap)

- **Sesame oil is a finishing oil.** Never in the cube, never in the pan with heat on.
- **Garlic rests 10 min after crushing** for allicin formation — or use a [Sheet 12 rested-garlic cube](./freeze-system.md).
- **Dry brine is the quick mode.** [Wet brine (Sheet 07)](./protocol-tofu.md) is for batch / max texture.
- **Tofu pulse: 2× / week ceiling.**
