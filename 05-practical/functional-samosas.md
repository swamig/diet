# Functional Samosas (Air-Fried Patty Variant)

This is an elite-level modification of the standard **[Vegan Protein Patties](vegan-protein-patties.md)**. By leveraging the "Flash Roast" rule, you can create a high-protein, crispy "Samosa" without deep-frying, while preserving the fragile Resistant Starch Type 3 (RS3) in the filling.

## The Biochemical Objective
*   **The Wrapper:** Must provide structure and crunch without introducing high-glycemic white flour (Maida).
*   **The Filling:** Must remain below the **130°F Thermal Ceiling** during cooking to prevent the RS3 crystals from melting back into simple starch.

---

## Option 1: The "Double-Protein" Wrap (Primary)
*The most functional, lowest-glycemic option. The wrapper is pure protein, leaving the chilled chickpeas as the sole source of carbohydrates.*

### The Components:
1.  **The Wrapper (Patty Dough):** Take a portion of your raw **[Vegan Protein Patty](vegan-protein-patties.md)** dough (rich in Vital Wheat Gluten and Pea Protein).
2.  **The Filling (RS3 Core):** Use cold, mashed **[Chickpeas](protocol-chickpeas.md)** straight from the fridge (must have completed their 24-hour chill). Mix heavily with grated fresh ginger, ajwain, and nigella to ensure digestion.

### The Execution:
1.  Roll the raw Patty dough out between two pieces of parchment paper into paper-thin circles.
2.  Place a spoonful of the **cold** chickpea filling in the center.
3.  Fold the dough over and pinch the edges tight to form a triangle or half-moon.
4.  Lightly spray the exterior with **Avocado Oil** (handles 400°F without oxidizing).
5.  **The Flash-Fry:** Place in the Air Fryer at **400°F (200°C) for exactly 7-8 minutes**.

### The Mechanism:
Because the skin contains Vital Wheat Gluten, it crisps into a "meat-like" shell under high, dry heat. Because the filling started at 38°F (fridge temp), the short 7-minute blast is enough to cook the skin without allowing the internal core to breach 130°F, preserving the RS3.

---

## Option 2: The "Besan" Skin (Backup)
*Use this if you want a more traditional "pastry" texture or if you are out of vital wheat gluten. This wrapper uses complex carbohydrates rather than pure protein.*

### The Components:
1.  **The Wrapper:** Mix 1 cup of **Besan (Chickpea Flour)** with 1/4 tsp Ajwain, a pinch of turmeric, and enough **[Aquafaba](sunday-prep.md#block-2-protein-batch-25-min)** (reserved chickpea liquid) to form a stiff dough.
2.  **The Filling:** Same as Option 1 (Cold, pre-chilled chickpeas or leftover patty dough).

### The Execution:
1.  Roll the Besan dough out very thin. The Aquafaba acts as an elite emulsifier to give it "snap."
2.  Stuff with the cold filling and fold into triangles.
3.  Spray with Avocado Oil.
4.  **The Flash-Fry:** Air Fry at **380°F (190°C) for 8-10 minutes**.

### The Mechanism:
While Besan has a much lower glycemic index than white flour, it is not RS3. The Aquafaba binding allows the Air Fryer to create a crunchy exterior without the oxidized lipids (AGEs) created by deep-frying in seed oils.
