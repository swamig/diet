# Functional Samosas (Air-Fried Patty Variant)

This is an elite-level modification of the standard **[Vegan Protein Patties](vegan-protein-patties.md)**. By leveraging the "Flash Roast" rule, you can create a high-protein, crispy "Samosa" without deep-frying, while preserving the fragile Resistant Starch Type 3 (RS3) in the filling.

## The Biochemical Objective
*   **The Wrapper:** Must provide structure and crunch without introducing high-glycemic white flour (Maida).
*   **The Filling:** Must remain below the **130°F Thermal Ceiling** during cooking to prevent the RS3 crystals from melting back into simple starch.

---

## Option 1: The "Pure Protein" Skin (Seitan Hybrid)
*The most functional, lowest-glycemic option. The wrapper is essentially 90% protein, leaving the chilled chickpeas as the sole source of carbohydrates.*

### The Components:
1.  **The Wrapper (90/10 Rule):** Mix **90% Vital Wheat Gluten (VWG)** and **10% Besan (Chickpea Flour)**. *Why 10% Besan? Pure VWG air-fries into a leathery tire. The small amount of Besan "softens" the gluten matrix, creating a crispy, pastry-like snap.*
2.  **The Binders:** Add 1 tsp Avocado Oil and hydrate with **Aquafaba** or water until a stiff dough forms. 
3.  **The Filling (RS3 Core):** Use cold, mashed **[Chickpeas](protocol-chickpeas.md)** straight from the fridge (must have completed their 24-hour chill). Fold in **1/4 cup pre-chilled Green Peas** (adds authentic sweetness and texture). Mix heavily with grated fresh ginger, ajwain, and nigella to ensure digestion. *Mandatory:* If using frozen peas, blanch and chill them completely before mixing; hot peas will melt the RS3 core during the air-fry.

### The Execution:
1.  **The Gluten Rest (CRITICAL):** You must let the wrapper dough rest for **30-60 minutes**. If you try to roll it immediately, the gluten will snap back like a rubber band.
2.  Roll the rested dough out between two pieces of parchment paper into paper-thin circles.
3.  Place a spoonful of the **cold** chickpea filling in the center.
4.  Fold the dough over and pinch the edges tight to form a triangle or half-moon.
5.  Lightly spray the exterior with **Avocado Oil** (handles 400°F without oxidizing).
6.  **The Flash-Fry:** Place in the Air Fryer at **400°F (200°C) for exactly 7-8 minutes**.

### The Mechanism:
Because the skin is mostly Vital Wheat Gluten, it crisps into a "meat-like" shell under high, dry heat while delivering nearly 25g of protein per serving. Because the filling started at 38°F (fridge temp), the short 7-minute blast is enough to cook the skin without allowing the internal core to breach 130°F, preserving the RS3.

## Option 3: The Elastic Gluten-Free Skin (Performance GF)
*The most rollable GF option. Uses psyllium husk to mimic the elasticity of gluten while providing massive fiber for gut motility.*

### The Components:
1.  **The Wrapper:** 1 cup **Besan (Chickpea Flour)** + 2 tbsp **Psyllium Husk Powder** + 1/4 tsp Ajwain + pinch of turmeric.
2.  **The Binders:** 1 tbsp Avocado Oil and hydrate with warm water or **[Aquafaba](sunday-prep.md#block-2-protein-batch-25-min)**.
3.  **The Filling:** Same as Option 1 (Cold RS3 Chickpea mash with pre-chilled peas).

### The Execution:
1.  **The Gel Hydration:** Once mixed, let the dough sit for **10 minutes**. The psyllium husk needs time to fully hydrate and create the "elastic gel" that holds the GF dough together.
2.  Roll the dough very thin between two pieces of parchment paper. Because of the psyllium, it will be flexible enough to fold without cracking.
3.  **The Flash-Fry:** Air Fry at **380°F (190°C) for 10-12 minutes**. 

---

## The Gluten Cycling Rule (Optional Advance Protocol)
Because Vital Wheat Gluten is a large, complex protein, consuming high doses daily can occasionally lead to low-grade gut inflammation (Zonulin signaling). 

*   **The Strategy:** Alternate between **Option 1 (High Protein)** and **Option 3 (Gluten-Free)** throughout the week.
*   **Best For:** Use Option 1 on **Heavy Lifting (M/W/F)** days to maximize protein efficiency. Use Option 3 on **Rest/Recovery (Tue/Thu/Sat)** days to provide a "clearance window" for the gut lining.
