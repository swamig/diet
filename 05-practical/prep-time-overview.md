# Prep Time Overview

Total time commitment for the protocol once the batch system is running. No daily cooking. No weekly cooking. Everything flows from a 3-month batch session.

---

## Summary Table

| Cadence | Who | Time | What |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Daily** | Owner | ~3 min active + 10 min passive | Garlic rest, cube pull, finish and plate |
| **Weekly** | Owner | ~25 min | Salad + recovery bowl infrastructure + thaw-refreeze RS3 boost |
| **Wednesday night** | Owner | 2 min | Pull next 3 days from freezer to fridge |
| **Every 3 months** | Owner + Helper | ~7 hours (Day 0) + 20 min (Day 1) | Full batch — see [Batch Prep SOP](sop-batch-prep.md) |

## Daily Prep (~8 min active, 10 min passive)

The 10-minute garlic rest is passive — start it, walk away, come back.

### Morning (07:15) — ~3 min active
1. Boil water (kettle).
2. Pour into cup with morning activation pack (ajwain or jeera — pre-portioned Sunday). Steep 5 min. (passive)
3. Prepare matcha: sift 1 tsp matcha into bowl or cup. Add 2 oz water at ~175°F (not boiling — boiling denatures catechins). Whisk or shake until no lumps. Add remaining water/milk.
4. Take with morning supplements per daily stack.

**Total morning active: ~3 min.** Steep and matcha whisk are passive overlaps.

### Meal Prep (12:00) — ~5 min active, 10 min passive
1. Crush 1–2 garlic cloves. Set on counter. **Start 10-min timer.** (passive — walk away)
2. Pull from fridge: rested garlic cube, chutney cube, imli cube, muddle cube.
3. Pull samosa parcel or bean portion from fridge. Set out.

**At 10 min:**
4. Warm beans/filling in pan to 130°F max.
5. Fold in rested garlic cube + day-of jar spices.
6. Off heat: drizzle EVOO + pinch black salt.
7. Plate. Fresh cilantro + lemon squeeze at table.

**If searing a patty or tofu:** Same garlic bloom → bloom in avocado oil → sear 60–90 sec per side → lemon over hot surface.

**Total meal active: ~5 min.** Timer handles the garlic window.

### Recovery Bowl (17:50) — ~1 min active
Everything pre-portioned Sunday. Pure assembly:
1. Scoop Greek yogurt into bowl (fresh, weekly purchase).
2. Add pre-portioned blueberry container (already thawed in fridge since Sunday).
3. Add pre-portioned casein jar.
4. Stir in 2 tbsp black bean liquid (thawed cube from fridge).
5. Combine and eat immediately — do not pre-mix and store.

**Total daily active: ~9 min across all three meals.**

## Weekly Prep (~20 min, owner only — Sunday)

### Sunday Morning (5 min — RS3 Boost)
Pull full week supply of beans and samosa filling from chest freezer → batch fridge. Let thaw fully. Refreeze Sunday night except Mon/Tue portions. See [Freeze System — Sunday Morning Pull](freeze-system.md#sunday-morning-pull--refreeze-5-min--rs3-boost).

### Sunday Salad + Recovery Infrastructure (~20 min)
1. Wash and spin greens. Store with paper towel in airtight container.
2. Chop green onions. Portion into small container.
3. Pre-portion 5 seed rotation containers (sesame / basil seeds / walnuts / chia / pumpkin).
4. Portion sauerkraut into 5 small cups.
5. Portion 7 daily spice jars (turmeric, cumin, coriander, Kashmiri chili).
6. Portion 7 morning activation packs (ajwain / jeera rotation).
7. **Recovery bowl pre-portion:** Divide 375g frozen blueberries into 5 × 8oz containers → fridge to thaw over the week.
8. **Casein pre-portion:** Measure 1 scoop casein into each of 5 dry jars. Seal.
9. Pull 1 black bean liquid cube to fridge to thaw for Monday bowl. (Pull next day's cube each evening.)

**Greek yogurt:** Fresh purchase weekly — no prep. Just keep stocked.

That is the entire Sunday commitment. No cooking.

### Wednesday Night (2 min)
Pull from freezer to fridge in one trip:
- [ ] 3 patties or samosa parcels
- [ ] 1 bean portion (black beans or chickpeas)
- [ ] 3 chutney cubes
- [ ] 3 imli cubes
- [ ] 3 mint muddle cubes
- [ ] 1 curry leaf tadka cube per day

## 3-Month Batch (~7 hours Day 0, 20 min Day 1)

One session covers the entire quarter. See [Batch Prep SOP](sop-batch-prep.md) for the full procedure.

### What's completed in one session:

| Category | Items |
|---|---|
| **Proteins** | Chickpeas (whole + mashed), black beans (whole + mashed), lentils, tofu slabs, vegan protein patties |
| **Samosa system** | Filling variants A/B/C/D (52 parcels), samosa dough disks (Options 1 + 2), day-of spice jars pre-measured |
| **Starches** | Quinoa, purple sweet potato |
| **Cube trays** | Aloe vera (5 trays), lemon juice (4 trays), imli (1 tray), aquafaba (1 tray), black bean liquid, ginger (2 trays), rested garlic (1 tray), fenugreek paste (1 tray), mint muddle (2 trays), cilantro/mint chutney (1 tray) |
| **Topical** | Aloe scalp gel (glass jar), rosemary oil infusion (start), fenugreek paste hair (from cube batch) |

### What is NOT done on batch day (separate owner sessions):
| Item | Cadence | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Toum emulsification | Monthly | 20 min |
| Curry leaf tadka | Monthly | 15 min |
| Coconut chutney refresh | Every 2–3 weeks | 10 min |
| Cilantro/mint chutney refresh | Every 2–3 weeks | 10 min |
| Samosa dough refresh (batch 2) | At 8-week mark | 20 min |

## Owner vs. Helper Split (Batch Day)

| Task | Owner | Helper |
|---|---|---|
| Stove (4 burners, oven, monitoring, skimming, portioning) | — | ✓ |
| Blender run (chutneys, muddle, fenugreek, ginger, aloe) | ✓ | — |
| Mixer run (samosa dough T+2:00, patty dough T+3:30) | ✓ | — |
| Cube trays (aloe, lemon, imli, aquafaba, black bean liquid) | ✓ | — |
| Samosa filling assembly + day-of jar pre-measure | ✓ | — |
| Skincare batch (aloe scalp gel, rosemary oil) | ✓ | — |
| Bean assembly at 130°F — portioning into bags | Both | Both |
| Sweet potato (oven, passive during simmer) | — | ✓ monitors |
| Cleanup | Both | Both |

**Owner active time on batch day: ~3 hours.**  
**Helper active time on batch day: ~6.5 hours** (mostly passive monitoring after T+0:20 simmer begins).
