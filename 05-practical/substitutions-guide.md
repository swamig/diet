# Substitutions Guide

[Back to Practical Logistics](README.md) | [Back to Manual](../README.md)

Use substitutions by role, not by taste alone. The goal is to preserve the signaling logic of the protocol even when the exact ingredient is unavailable.

## Daily Anchors

| If You Are Missing | Best Substitute | Keep the Same Logic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Microgreens** | Watercress, arugula, or radish sprouts | Keep a low-oxalate phytonutrient garnish in the salad layer. |
| **Cilantro** | Parsley | Keep a fresh herb layer at the end of the meal. |
| **Sauerkraut** | Kimchi or another unsweetened ferment | Keep the daily ferment dose small and consistent. |
| **Broccoli** | Broccoli slaw, chopped Brussels sprouts, or cabbage + mustard powder | Preserve the chop-and-rest crucifer logic. |
| **Greek yogurt** | Skyr or a high-protein unsweetened yogurt | Keep the recovery bowl protein + calcium role intact. |

## Protein System

| If You Are Missing | Best Substitute | Keep the Same Logic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vegan patties** | Tofu | Keep the meal anchored by protein, spices, and broccoli. |
| **Tofu** | Vegan patties or lentils | Maintain the protein-first structure of the main meal. |
| **Chickpeas** | Black beans or lentils | Preserve the chilled legume role when possible. |
| **Casein** | Additional Greek yogurt or a similar slow-digesting protein | Keep the recovery bowl anchored and not purely fruit-based. |

## Greens, Seeds, and Vascular Layer

| If You Are Missing | Best Substitute | Keep the Same Logic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Arugula** | Watercress, kale, or romaine | Preserve the bitter / leafy salad base. |
| **Pumpkin seeds** | Sesame seeds or walnuts | Keep a mineral or fat layer in the salad. |
| **Walnuts** | Pumpkin seeds or sesame seeds | Preserve crunch and the fat/mineral layer. |
| **Pomegranate** | Berries or extra microgreens | Use this only when the day calls for extra vascular support. |

## Spice and Infusion Layer

| If You Are Missing | Best Substitute | Keep the Same Logic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ajwain water** | Jeera water | Keep a warm digestive hydration block. |
| **Jeera water** | Ajwain water | Use the gentler option if digestion is already sensitive. |
| **Tulsi infusion** | Saffron infusion | Keep the nighttime downshift, but respect saffron frequency. |
| **Saffron infusion** | Tulsi infusion | Preserve the evening parasympathetic cue. |

## Rules for Safe Substitutions

- Do not replace a protein anchor with a carb-only option.
- Do not replace a crucifer with a random vegetable and assume the same signaling result.
- Do not stack multiple substitutions that all increase oxalate, antimicrobial intensity, or stimulant load on the same day.
- When in doubt, simplify the day and preserve the anchors: protein, broccoli/crucifer logic, turmeric + black pepper, ferment, microgreens/cilantro, recovery bowl.

- Stacking multiple higher-risk substitutions on the same day makes troubleshooting harder.
