# Master Freeze System

Single reference for everything frozen in the protocol — format, shelf life, thaw method, and what to add fresh at serving. The goal is zero daily decision-making.

---

## Batch Cadence Overview

| Cadence | Items |
|---|---|
| **Every 2–3 weeks** | Patties, Coconut chutney base, Cilantro/mint chutney |
| **Monthly** | Curry leaf tadka, Mint muddle, Rested garlic, Fenugreek paste (hair) |
| **Every 3 months** | Aloe vera, Black beans, Chickpeas, Tofu, Purple sweet potato, Imli |

---

## Tray Count

**4 ice cube trays.** Trays are reused — freeze overnight, transfer to bag/container, tray is free.

| Tray items | Non-tray items |
|---|---|
| Coconut chutney base | Patties (parchment + bag) |
| Cilantro/mint chutney | Tofu (slabs, flat bag) |

| Rested garlic | Chickpeas (1.5-cup portion bags) |

| Fenugreek paste (hair) | Refried mash (flat slabs, bag) |
| Imli | Samosa filling (tray or portion bag) |

## Thaw + Finish Guide

### Patties
- **Thaw:** Pull 3 from freezer to fridge Sunday night. Pull next 3 Wednesday night.
- **Add fresh at serving:**
  - Crush 1–2 garlic cloves → rest 10 min (allicin — destroyed by steaming, must be fresh daily)
  - Bloom rested garlic + daily spice jar in avocado oil
  - Sear patty 60–90 sec per side
  - Squeeze 1/2 lemon directly over hot patty before serving

### Black Beans (whole, cooked)
- **Thaw:** Pull 1.5-cup portion to fridge Sunday night. Pull next portion Wednesday night.
- **Add fresh at serving (Cold Mash method):**
  - Mash cold beans with 1/4 cup aquafaba
  - Bloom methi + cumin seeds dry, then add rested garlic + ginger
  - Flash bloom powders (achiote, chaat masala, cinnamon, paprika) 10–20 sec
  - Fold in mashed beans + ACV to stop roasting
  - Warm to 130°F max
  - Finish: drizzle EVOO, pinch black salt, fresh cilantro (never frozen with the beans)

### Chickpeas (whole, cooked)
- **Thaw:** Same cadence as black beans — pull Sunday + Wednesday.
- **Add fresh at serving:**
  - For croutons: toss cold chickpeas in avocado oil + turmeric + cumin + black pepper → flash roast 380°F, 10–12 min
  - For patties/samosa filling: use cold directly per recipe
  - For main legume: warm to 130°F, fold in rested garlic + lemon juice

### Tofu
- **Thaw:** Fridge overnight OR sealed bag in cold water 30–60 min. Self-presses as it thaws — squeeze out remaining water before use.
- **Add fresh at serving:**
  - Crush 1–2 garlic cloves → rest 10 min
  - Bloom rested garlic + chili in avocado oil
  - Sear tofu on high heat for crust
  - Add marinade (teriyaki, miso, or spice stack) directly into the pan

### Purple Sweet Potato
- **Thaw:** Fridge overnight. Use cold for Chaat Base — do not reheat unless necessary.
- **Add fresh at serving:**
  - Toss cold cubes with 1/4–1/2 tsp chaat masala + squeeze of lemon juice (Vitamin C anchor for iron absorption)
  - Optional: pinch black salt

### Coconut Chutney Base
- **Thaw:** 1–2 cubes at room temp 15 min, or drop into a small bowl.
- **Add fresh at serving:**
  - Drop 1 curry leaf tadka cube into a warm pan → let melt → pour over thawed chutney base
  - Stir well — texture returns fully
  - Do not microwave the chutney base itself

### Curry Leaf Tadka
- **Thaw:** Drop directly into a warm pan. Melts in seconds.
- **Use:** Pour over thawed coconut chutney, or finish any dish requiring tempering.

### Cilantro/Mint Chutney
- **Thaw:** 1–2 cubes at room temp 15 min.
- **Add fresh at serving:** Ready to use — acid lock (lemon) was applied before freezing. Add a fresh squeeze of lemon if flavor seems flat.

### Imli (Tamarind)
- **Thaw:** 1–2 cubes at room temp 15 min.
- **Add fresh at serving:**
  - Stir in 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder + pinch black salt
  - These were deliberately excluded before freezing (osmosis rule)

### Rested Garlic Cubes
- **Thaw:** Drop into a cold pan or bowl — do not apply heat. Allicin already formed.
- **Use:** As base for any recipe requiring rested garlic. No second 10-min rest needed.

### Mint Muddle
- **Thaw:** Drop 1 cube into glass. Thaws in cold water in 5–10 min.
- **Add fresh at serving:**
  - Add roasted cumin powder, black salt, ACV, water/sparkling water
  - These were excluded before freezing (osmosis rule — salt bleeds water from paste even frozen)

### Samosa Filling (RS3 Core)
- **Thaw:** Fridge overnight or room temp 20 min. Use cold — do not microwave.
- **Add fresh at serving:** Assemble cold into fresh-rolled dough immediately before air frying.

## Wednesday Night Pull Checklist (2 min)

Pull all of the following from freezer to fridge in one trip:
- [ ] 3 patties
- [ ] 1 black bean portion (1.5 cups)
- [ ] 1 chickpea portion if needed
- [ ] 3 coconut chutney base cubes
- [ ] 3 cilantro/mint chutney cubes
- [ ] 3 imli cubes
- [ ] 3 mint muddle cubes
- [ ] 1 curry leaf tadka cube per day needed
