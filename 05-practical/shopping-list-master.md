# Consolidated Master Shopping List

## Buy Once (Non-Perishables)
These will last months.

**Spices (Buy Full Jars):**
*   Turmeric powder (at least 120g)
*   Whole black peppercorns (grind fresh)
*   Cumin seeds (Jeera)
*   Coriander powder (Dhania)
*   Kashmiri Chili powder (mild, for color)
*   Nigella seeds (Black Jeera)
*   Ajwain seeds
*   Ceylon cinnamon (powder or sticks)
*   Whole cloves
*   Dried tulsi (Holy Basil)
*   Mustard powder
*   Saffron threads (High quality, ~1g vial)
*   Unrefined Sea Salt

**Pantry:**
*   Extra virgin olive oil (750ml)
*   Balsamic vinegar
*   Dijon Mustard (sugar-free)
*   Tomato Puree (or finely chopped tomatoes)
*   Raw cacao nibs (250-300g)
*   Casein protein powder (large tub)
*   Quinoa (2-2.5 kg dry)
*   Lentils (1.5 kg dry)
*   Pumpkin seeds
*   Sesame seeds
*   Walnuts
*   Chia seeds

## Buy Weekly (Fresh Produce)
This must be refreshed weekly to maintain nutrient density.

**Greens & Herbs:**
*   **Microgreens (4-5 small boxes/week)** - Essential. Do not skip.
*   **Fresh Cilantro (2-3 large bunches)** - Essential daily garnish.
*   Arugula (2 large boxes)
*   Kale (1 large bunch)
*   Watercress (1 bunch)
*   Romaine (1 head)
*   Green onions (2 bunches)
*   Fresh Ginger root
*   Lemons (4-5)

**Cruciferous:**
*   Broccoli (1.2-1.5 kg) - Approx 3-4 large heads

**Proteins & Dairy:**
*   Chicken breast (approx 1 kg/week or buy bulk and freeze)
*   Tofu (3-4 blocks/week)
*   Greek yogurt (2-3 large tubs/week)

**Ferments:**
*   Sauerkraut or Kimchi (1 large jar lasts ~2 weeks)

## Buy Twice Weekly (Optional for Freshness)
*   Microgreens (if shelf life is short)
*   Watercress (wilts fast)

## Freezer Stash (Backup)
*   Frozen blueberries (2 kg bag)
*   Frozen pomegranate seeds
*   Portioned chicken breasts
