# Protocol Tofu

Tofu runs 2x/week in the rotation. It is the primary vehicle for the High TRP Activation pulse (capsaicin + menthol synergy) and the allicin flash-sear. This page consolidates sourcing, storage, prep, and execution into a single reference.

---

## 1. Sourcing

- **Type:** Extra-firm tofu only. Silken and soft tofu do not hold structure through the freeze-thaw cycle or the flash sear.
- **Size:** 200g per serving. Buy 3–4 blocks per weekly shop.
- **Label check:** Water-packed preferred. Avoid blocks with gums (xanthan, carrageenan) in the ingredient list — they interfere with texture after freeze-thaw.

---

## 2. Storage — Freeze Protocol (Sunday)

Freezing is not optional — it is the structural upgrade that makes tofu worth eating on this protocol.

1. Open block, drain, pat dry with paper towels
2. Slice into **1-inch slabs** — slabs thaw faster and portion better than a whole block
3. Lay flat in a **freezer-safe zip-lock bag** — single layer, no stacking before frozen
4. Freeze immediately — shelf life frozen: **3–4 months**

**Why freeze:** ice crystals rupture the protein matrix, creating a spongier, more porous structure [[PMC10494460]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#11-operational-mechanisms-bio-enhancement--degradation). On thaw: water comes out on its own (faster pressing), marinade penetrates deeper, more surface area for the allicin crust during the flash sear. Strictly better than fridge-only storage.

**Fridge-only fallback (short week):** press water out, slice into slabs, store submerged in fresh water, change water daily. Use within 4–5 days. No freeze-upgrade benefits.

---

## 3. Day-Of Prep Sequence

### Step 1 — Thaw
- **Fridge overnight** (preferred) — slow thaw preserves texture better than fast methods
- **Cold water fast thaw** — submerge sealed bag in cold water 30–60 min

### Step 2 — Salt Brine
After thawing, before pressing or marinating:

1. Bring a pot of water to a **full boil**
2. Add **1 tsp salt per 4 cups water**
3. Submerge tofu slabs for **3–5 minutes**
4. Remove, transfer to a clean surface

**Why:** the boil expels residual ice-crystal water and opens the pores fully; the salt penetrates the interior, seasoning from inside rather than just the surface. Net result: seasoned through, drier surface, superior crust on the flash sear.

### Step 3 — Press
After brine, press briefly — 5 minutes with a clean towel and a heavy pan on top. Most water has already exited from the brine step; this is a final dry.

### Step 4 — Marinate (Optional)
The porous post-freeze, post-brine structure absorbs marinade in **15–20 minutes** (vs. hours for unfreeze tofu). Simple options:
- **Tamari + ginger cube + ACV** — 2 tbsp tamari, 1 ginger cube (melted), 1 tsp ACV, 15 min
- **Dry rub** — protocol spice mix (turmeric, cumin, coriander, Kashmiri chili) rubbed directly onto slab before sear — no wait time needed

## 4. Execution — The Flash Sear

See full workflow: [High-Signaling Garlic-Chili Pulse](garlic-chili-oil.md)

**Summary:**
1. **00:00** — Crush 2–3 garlic cloves. Rest **10 minutes** (allicin formation — non-negotiable) [[Song & Milner, 2001]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#3-allicin-activation-the-10-minute-garlic-rule)
2. **00:10** — Cold start: add 2 tbsp avocado oil + methi seeds + chili + rested garlic to cold pan
3. **00:11** — Low heat bloom: 5 min until garlic barely sizzles — capsaicin, sulfur, and fenugreek extract into fat [[Block, 1985]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#3-allicin-activation-the-10-minute-garlic-rule)
4. **00:16** — Turn to medium-high. Add tofu slabs. Sear **60–90 sec per side** until golden crust forms
5. **00:25** — Off heat. Tear 5–6 fresh Mentha arvensis leaves over hot tofu — residual heat releases menthol without destroying it [[Peter, 2006]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#11-operational-mechanisms-bio-enhancement--degradation)
6. **00:26** — Serve immediately with Peppermint Jaljeera

**Variants:**
- **Wet Curry:** add 2–3 tbsp coconut milk at step 5, stir 30–60 sec into glaze. Reduce salad dressing oil by 1 tbsp (Lipid Substitution Rule)
- **Teriyaki Pulse:** see [garlic-chili-oil.md — Teriyaki Variant](garlic-chili-oil.md#the-teriyaki-pulse-variant-optional)

## 5. Protocol Role

**Frequency:** 2x/week (rotation days)

**The High TRP Activation Pulse:** tofu days use extra Thai chilies in the sear. The combined Jaljeera chili + sear chili + menthol creates the weekly peak capsaicin-menthol synergy — combining TRPV1 (capsaicin) and TRPM8 (menthol) activation massively amplifies anti-inflammatory cytokine downregulation vs. isolated exposure [[Nutrients, 2025]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#1-trp-receptor-cross-talk-menthol--capsaicin).

**Iron gate:** tofu contains no calcium — it does not trigger the dairy/calcium iron blockade at 12:00. Safe alongside the legume + Vitamin C iron absorption stack [[Zijp, 2000]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#6-iron-absorption--tannin-blockades-the-matcha-rule).

**Protein:** 200g extra-firm tofu delivers ~17–20g complete protein (all essential amino acids). Pairs with the 25g pea protein post-workout or recovery bowl — do not stack both in the same meal window.

## 6. Reheating

- **Pan (preferred):** low–medium heat, 5–8 min, stir/flip frequently. Add splash of aquafaba or water to prevent sticking.
- **Do not microwave on high** — uneven heat exceeds the RS3 ceiling in adjacent grain components and ruins the sear crust texture.
