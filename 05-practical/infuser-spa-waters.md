# Infuser Spa Waters

Protocol-compatible cold infusions to help reach the 3–4L daily fluid target (training days) and 2.5–3L (rest days). Use a fruit/herb infuser bottle or pitcher — steep cold in the fridge 1–2h, or use immediately for milder flavor.

## Protocol-Compatible Waters

| Water | Ingredients | Best Window | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Cucumber + Mint** | 1 cucumber sliced + 10 mint leaves | All day | Zero conflicts. Classic cooling water. Fill and refill all day. Best daily driver. |
| **Fennel + Lemon** | 1 tsp fennel seeds + lemon slices | Between meals | Pro-kinetic and digestive. Good in the 12:00–17:50 gap. |
| **Tulsi + Lemon** | 8–10 fresh tulsi leaves + lemon slices | 11:00–16:00 | Adaptogenic daytime water. Distinct from the 21:00 night infusion role — no cycling conflict at this dose. |
| **Rose + Cardamom** | 10 dried rose petals + 2 cardamom pods cracked | Afternoon | Aromatic phantom sweetness effect. Anti-inflammatory. Zero protocol conflicts. |
| **Hibiscus cold brew** | 1 tsp dried hibiscus + cold water, steep 2h | Before 10:00 or after 14:30 | Polyphenol-rich, tart. Has mild tannins — keep away from 12:00 meal (Vitamin C doing iron-absorption work there). |
| **Ginger + Lemon** | Thin ginger coins + lemon slices | Morning or midday | Mild pro-kinetic cold version. Lower potency than the 09:15 hot pulse — not a replacement, just a hydration enhancer. |

## The Tannin Rule

**Avoid hibiscus (and rosehip) in the 11:00–13:00 window.** This is when Vitamin C from the 12:00 meal is actively pulling non-heme iron from your legumes. Tannins block iron absorption at the same binding sites — you undo the Vitamin C work.

Consume hibiscus water before 10:00 (with the morning tannin window) or after 14:30 (well past the 12:00 iron window).

## Infuser Tips

- **Cold steep:** Fill infuser, refrigerate 1–2h for full flavor. Good morning habit — prep at 07:00, ready at 09:00.
- **Direct use:** For cucumber and mint, cold water is effective immediately (high water content releases quickly).
- **Refill rule:** One fill of ingredients → 2–3 refills of water. Discard after 8h (fenugreek, tulsi) or 24h (cucumber, fennel).
- **No sweeteners:** The phantom sweetness from cardamom + rose or fennel is the protocol approach — no honey, no stevia.

## Hydration Targets

- **Training days:** 3–4L total daily fluid (water + infuser water + herbal teas + meals)
- **Rest days:** 2.5–3L total
- **Creatine days:** Stay at the upper end — creatine draws water into muscle cells; under-hydration increases cramping risk

## Daily Driver

**Cucumber + Mint** — zero conflicts, all-day. Fill the infuser in the morning, carry it, refill throughout the day. Everything else is rotation or occasion-specific.
