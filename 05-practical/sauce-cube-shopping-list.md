# Sauce Cube Shopping List

A consolidated list across the three sauce-cube recipes — [Sesame Sauce Cubes](./sesame-sauce-cubes.md), [Creamy Coconut Protein Cubes](./coconut-protein-cubes.md), and [Pad Thai Peanut Cubes](./pad-thai-peanut-cubes.md). The cubes share most of their pantry, so shopping per-recipe wastes both money and shelf space. Use this list for a single store visit at one of two scales — single batch of each (one round of all three cubes) or 3-month Vitamix batch of each.

Companion to [Master Batch Prep](./master-batch-prep.md), which covers the legumes / grains / produce side of the protocol. The design principles behind every line below live in the [Sauce Cubing Master Guide](./sauce-cubing-master-guide.md).

---

## Quick math — which scale do I shop?

| Scale | Total cubes | Covers (moderate use) | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Single batch of each** | ~44 cubes (sesame ~20 mini + coconut ~16 + peanut ~12) | ~3–4 weeks | First try · small household · testing the system |
| **3-month Vitamix batch of each** | ~216 cubes (sesame ~24 + coconut ~96 + peanut ~96) | ~3 months at 2 cubes × 5 meals/week | Committed cube-system households · one big shop |

**Pick-one-cube approach:** if you only want to start with the peanut cube, jump to [pad-thai-peanut-cubes.md](./pad-thai-peanut-cubes.md) and use its standalone shopping list — no need to over-buy aromatics for sauces you're not making.

## Shopping list — Single batch of each (~44 cubes total)

Organized by pantry category. Each row aggregates across the three cubes; the **Breakdown** column shows which recipe consumes what.

### Liquids & condiments

| Item | Amount | Breakdown |
|---|---|---|
| Full-fat coconut milk | 1 can (~400 mL) | Coconut: 1 can |
| Natural roasted peanut butter | ¾ cup (~190 g) | Peanut: ¾ cup |
| Low-sodium tamari / soy sauce | ~7 tbsp | Sesame 2 + Coconut 2 + Peanut 3 |
| Limes (fresh) | ~4 medium | Sesame 2 tbsp + Coconut 2 tbsp + Peanut 3 tbsp |
| Apple cider vinegar (raw) | 2 tbsp | Peanut only |
| Rice vinegar (unseasoned) | 1 tbsp | Sesame only |
| Oyster sauce **OR** 1 veg-oyster cube ([Sheet 22](./oyster-sauce-vegetarian.md)) | 1 tbsp | Sesame only |
| Sriracha **OR** 1 sriracha cube ([Sheet 23](./sriracha-homemade.md)) | 1–2 tsp | Sesame only |

### Sweetener

| Item | Amount | Breakdown |
|---|---|---|
| Medjool dates (preferred) **OR** honey | 3–5 dates *(or 1 tsp honey + 2 tbsp date paste)* | Sesame 1 tsp honey · Coconut 0 (curry paste covers it) · Peanut 2 dates |

### Aromatics

| Item | Amount | Breakdown |
|---|---|---|
| Fresh ginger | ~3 thumbs (~3 oz) | Sesame small · Coconut 1 thumb · Peanut 1 thumb |
| Garlic | 3 cloves | Peanut only — sesame's allicin comes from the rested-garlic cube ([freeze-system.md](./freeze-system.md)) |

### Umami / fermented

| Item | Amount | Breakdown |
|---|---|---|
| White miso (unpasteurized) | 2 tbsp | Coconut 1 tbsp + Peanut 1 tbsp · **both off-heat** to preserve LAB |
| Shiitake mushroom powder | 1 tsp | Peanut only |

### Spice base (Coconut cube)

| Item | Amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Red Thai curry paste | 2 tbsp | **Or** build-your-own: 1 tsp turmeric + 1 tsp cumin + 1 tsp coriander + ½ tsp cardamom + ¼ tsp cinnamon + 1 tsp lemongrass paste (optional) |

### Body / thickener

| Item | Amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Arrowroot powder | 1 tsp | Coconut only · freezes cleaner than cornstarch (no weeping on thaw) |
| Sea salt | ~1 tsp total | Coconut ½ tsp + Peanut up to ½ tsp |

### Bio-signal stack (now default — see [sauce-cubing-master-guide.md](./sauce-cubing-master-guide.md))

| Item | Amount | Breakdown |
|---|---|---|
| Coarse black pepper (whole peppercorns, crack fresh) | ~2 tsp ground worth | Sesame ½ + Coconut ½ + Peanut 1 — piperine absorption multiplier |
| Turmeric (ground) | ¼ tsp | Sesame only · paired with the pepper for curcumin synergy |
| Black mustard seeds (whole) | ½ tsp | Sesame only — dry-toast and grind |
| Sichuan peppercorn (whole) | 1 tsp | Sesame ½ + Peanut ½ — toast and grind yourself |
| Black garlic powder | 3 tsp | Sesame 1 + Coconut 1 + Peanut 1 — heat-stable SAC sulfur |
| Smoked paprika (Hungarian or Spanish) | 1 tsp | Peanut only |
| Thai bird's eye chilies (frozen whole, 3–6 mo) | ~5 chilies | Coconut 1 + Peanut 4 |
| *Optional* hemp hearts **OR** pea protein | 2 tbsp | Coconut satiety boost · skip if unwanted |

### Day-of finishers (NOT in cubes — buy once, keep stocked)

| Item | Amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Toasted sesame oil | 1 small bottle (250 mL) | **Off-heat drizzle · finishing oil only** — aromatics smoke off above 230 °F |
| Toasted sesame seeds (white + black mixed) | 1 jar | Sprinkle finish on sesame and peanut bowls |
| Fresh mint | 1 bunch | Rotate weekly — volatile menthol top notes |
| Fresh cilantro | 1 bunch | Rotate weekly — coconut and peanut bowls |
| Scallion | 1 bunch | Aromatic finish · all three cubes |
| Lime wedges | (covered in *Liquids* above) | Squeeze at the plate |
| *Optional:* nori strips, microgreens | small amounts | Iodine + glutamate · texture finish |
| Rested garlic cubes ([freeze-system.md](./freeze-system.md)) | should already be stocked | Sheet 12 protocol — allicin pulse, off-heat |

## Storage notes

- **Coconut milk** — pantry until opened. Freeze any leftover concentrate from a small batch in cube form.
- **Peanut butter** — fridge after opening for the standard 6-month shelf life on a single jar; freezer for the 6+ month batch reserves.
- **Tamari, ACV, rice vinegar** — pantry, dark cool spot. All shelf-stable for 1+ year sealed.
- **Fresh ginger** — freeze whole in a sealed bag (3-month) and grate from frozen ([freeze-system.md](./freeze-system.md)).
- **Garlic** — Sheet 12 rested-garlic cubes preferred over fresh for the day-of allicin pulse. Fresh cloves for the in-cube simmer (Peanut) only.
- **Thai bird's eye chilies** — freeze whole 3–6 months in a sealed bag ([freeze-system.md](./freeze-system.md)).
- **Miso (unpasteurized)** — fridge always. **Do NOT freeze** — kills the LAB cultures that are the entire point of cubing miso in off-heat.
- **Black garlic powder, smoked paprika, mustard seeds, Sichuan peppercorn, turmeric** — pantry, airtight, ~1 year before flavor dulls. Buy whole spices where possible and grind in small batches.
- **Sesame oil** — fridge after opening. Toasted aromatics oxidize at room temp within ~3 months once open.
- **Sesame seeds** — pantry sealed, fridge after opening for max aroma retention.

## Estimated cost (rough USD, bulk pantry pricing)

| Scale | Range |
|---|---|
| Single batch of all three | **~$25–35** |
| 3-month Vitamix batch of all three | **~$110–150** |
| **Per-cube cost** | **~$0.15–0.30** |

The bio-signal stack (black pepper, turmeric, mustard seeds, Sichuan peppercorn, black garlic powder, smoked paprika) is a **one-time pantry investment (~$30 upfront)** that lasts 6–12 months of cube production — amortize it across every batch.
