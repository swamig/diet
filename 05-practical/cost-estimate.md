## What Drives the Cost

The salad layer at $5.44/day is **53% of total daily food cost.** This is the trade-off for the most signal-dense meal in the protocol — 4 bacterial strains, sulforaphane, nitric oxide precursors, 4 classes of polyphenols, and direct vascular signaling in one bowl. Growing broccoli sprouts at home is the single highest-leverage cost reduction available.

The protein stack ($0.85 + $2.72 = $3.57) is the second largest category, now optimized with pea/rice blend replacing both whey and casein.
