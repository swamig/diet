# Topical Protocols (Hair, Face, Body)

Ayurvedic topical ingredients operate on the same signaling logic as the dietary protocol — anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and circulatory mechanisms — applied externally. This page encodes a zero-effort batch system for all three applications.

---

## The Ingredient Stack

| Ingredient | Hair | Face | Body | Active Mechanism |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fenugreek (soaked + ground) | Yes | No | No | Lecithin coats shaft, saponins anti-fungal (anti-dandruff), nicotinic acid improves scalp circulation |
| Amla powder | Yes | Yes | Yes | Highest natural Vitamin C + tannins — strengthens follicles, prevents breakage, brightens skin |
| Brahmi powder | Yes | No | No | Bacosides reduce scalp inflammation and cortisol-driven hair loss, strengthen shaft |
| Coconut oil | Yes | Sparingly | Yes | Only oil that penetrates the hair shaft — reduces protein loss by ~50%. Comedogenic risk on face — use minimally |
| Aloe vera gel | Yes | Yes | Yes | pH 4.5 matches hair and skin's natural acidity. Proteolytic enzymes repair dead cells, reduce dandruff and inflammation |
| Turmeric | Tiny pinch only | Yes | Yes | Anti-inflammatory, anti-acne, brightening. **Stains hair yellow — 1/8 tsp max for hair or omit entirely** |
| Besan (chickpea flour) | **No** | Yes | Yes | Exfoliant, oil absorption. Too drying for hair shaft |
| Rose water | No | Yes | Optional | pH balancing toner, anti-inflammatory |

## 1. Hair Mask Protocol

**Frequency:** 2x/week (Wednesday + Sunday).

**Leave time:** 30–45 minutes under a shower cap.

### Active Compounds Per Application
*   2 tbsp **Dry Hair Mix** (see batch prep below)
*   1 **Fenugreek Paste Cube** (thawed — see batch prep)
*   1 **Aloe Cube** (thawed)
*   1 tbsp **Coconut Oil** (melted)
*   Optional: 1/8 tsp turmeric (anti-inflammatory scalp pulse — omit if staining is a concern)

### Execution
1.  Thaw 1 fenugreek cube + 1 aloe cube (30 min at room temp or overnight in fridge).
2.  Combine with dry hair mix and coconut oil. Mix into a thick paste — add a splash of water if too stiff.
3.  Apply to scalp first (massage 2–3 min for circulatory activation), then work through lengths.
4.  Cover with shower cap. Leave 30–45 min.
5.  **Do not let the mask fully dry and harden** — dried paste causes breakage and tangles when removing. Rinse while still slightly damp.
6.  Rinse with cool water. Shampoo once if needed — avoid stripping with hot water.

## 2. Face Mask Protocol

**Frequency:** 2x/week.

**Leave time:** 10–15 minutes.

### Active Compounds Per Application
*   1 tsp **Dry Face Mix** (see batch prep below)
*   1 **Aloe Cube** (thawed) — or rose water to bind
*   Tiny drop of coconut oil (optional, for dry skin only)

### Execution
1.  Thaw 1 aloe cube.
2.  Mix dry face mix with aloe (and coconut oil if using) into a paste.
3.  Apply to clean, dry face. Avoid eye area.
4.  Leave 10–15 min. Rinse with cool water.
5.  No additional moisturizer needed if aloe cube was used — aloe seals the skin.

---

## 3. Body Ubtan Protocol

**Frequency:** 1x/week (pre-shower).

**Leave time:** 5–10 min, then scrub off in shower.

### Active Compounds Per Application
*   2 tbsp **Dry Body Mix** (see batch prep below)
*   1 **Aloe Cube** (thawed)
*   1 tbsp coconut oil or sesame oil

### Execution
1.  Mix dry body mix + aloe + oil into a thick paste.
2.  Apply to damp skin in circular motions (the besan acts as a physical exfoliant).
3.  Leave 5–10 min.
4.  Scrub off in shower with warm water. No soap needed on treated areas.

## 4. Zero-Effort Batch Prep System

All three protocols share the same architecture: **pre-mixed dry jars + frozen wet cubes**. Day-of prep is under 2 minutes.

### Dry Mix Jars (Shelf stable — 1 month)

**Dry Hair Mix jar:**
*   4 tbsp Amla powder
*   4 tbsp Brahmi powder (Bacopa monnieri) or Bhringraj powder (Eclipta alba) — pick one per jar, rotate monthly
*   2 tbsp Fenugreek powder (dry — backup if no frozen paste available)
*   Mix and store in a labeled airtight glass jar.

**Dry Face Mix jar:**
*   4 tbsp Besan (chickpea flour)
*   2 tbsp Amla powder
*   1 tsp Turmeric
*   Mix and store in a labeled airtight glass jar.

**Dry Body Mix jar:**
*   6 tbsp Besan
*   2 tbsp Amla powder
*   1.5 tsp Turmeric
*   Mix and store in a labeled airtight glass jar.

### Frozen Wet Cubes (Ice Cube Tray System)

**Fenugreek Paste Cubes (Hair only):**
1.  Soak 4 tbsp fenugreek seeds overnight in water.
2.  Drain and blend with just enough water to form a thick paste.
3.  Portion into ice cube tray (~1 tbsp per cube = 1 hair application).
4.  Freeze overnight, transfer to airtight container.
5.  **Shelf life frozen:** 4–6 weeks.
*   Why fresh-soaked beats dry powder: soaking activates mucilage and lecithin that coat and condition the shaft. Dry fenugreek powder provides fewer of these compounds.

**Aloe Vera Cubes (All three protocols):**
1.  **Drain the latex first:** Cut leaf at the base. Stand upright cut-end down in a glass for 10–15 min — yellow latex (aloin) drains out. Do not skip — aloin is bitter and irritates skin.
2.  Lay leaf flat. Slice off serrated edges on both sides.
3.  **Fillet the top skin:** Run knife just under the green surface and peel back. Clear gel is exposed underneath.
4.  Scoop out gel with a spoon — stay away from the skin edge where residual latex sits.
5.  Blend until completely smooth.
6.  Portion into ice cube tray (~1 tbsp per cube).
7.  Freeze overnight, transfer to airtight container.
8.  **Shelf life frozen:** 3–4 months.
*   1 cube = 1 application (hair, face, or body).

### Weekly Cadence

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| Wednesday | Hair + Face + Body combined (brief pre-sauna → Epsom bath → drain = rinse) |
| Sunday | Hair + Face combined (shower rinse) + check dry jar levels. Batch cubes if running low. |

## 5. Batch Prep by Cadence

### Every 3 Months — One-Time Setup

Make the dry jars and aloe cubes together in a single session (~20 min).

**Shopping quantities (3-month supply, 1 person):**

| Ingredient | Amount to buy | Used in |
|---|---|---|
| Amla powder | 200g | Hair + face + body dry mixes |
| Brahmi powder | 100g | Hair dry mix |
| Bhringraj powder | 100g | Hair dry mix (alternate monthly with brahmi) |
| Besan (chickpea flour) | 300g | Face + body dry mixes |
| Turmeric | 30g | Face + body dry mixes |
| Fenugreek seeds | 250g | Hair paste cubes (monthly batches) |
| Aloe vera (fresh leaves) | 5–6 large leaves | All three protocols |
| Coconut oil | 300ml | Hair + body |

**Dry jar prep (one session, lasts ~1 month each — refill from bulk stock):**

*Hair jar (makes ~3 months supply in bulk):*
- 12 tbsp Amla powder
- 12 tbsp Brahmi or Bhringraj powder (alternate each refill)
- 6 tbsp dry Fenugreek powder (backup only)
- Store in a large airtight jar. Scoop into a small working jar monthly.

*Face jar (makes ~3 months supply in bulk):*
- 12 tbsp Besan
- 6 tbsp Amla powder
- 1 tbsp Turmeric
- Store in a large airtight jar.

*Body jar (makes ~3 months supply in bulk):*
- 18 tbsp Besan
- 6 tbsp Amla powder
- 1.5 tbsp Turmeric
- Store in a large airtight jar.

**Aloe cube batch (one session, lasts 3 months):**
- Slice 3–4 large aloe leaves, scoop all gel, blend smooth
- Fill 1 ice cube tray (~1 tbsp per cube)
- 1 tray = ~14 cubes = ~3 weeks of use (hair 2x + face 2x + body 1x = 5 cubes/week)
- You need ~65 cubes for 3 months → fill tray 5 times over the session, or use 2–3 trays simultaneously
- Transfer frozen cubes to a single airtight bag labeled "Aloe"

### Monthly — Fenugreek Paste Cubes

*One session, ~10 min (soak overnight the day before):*
1. Soak 4 tbsp fenugreek seeds overnight.
2. Drain, blend with minimal water into thick paste.
3. Fill ice cube tray (~1 tbsp per cube = 1 hair application).
4. Freeze, transfer to bag.
5. **Yield:** ~12–14 cubes. At 1–2x/week hair mask = 6–10 week supply.
6. **Cadence:** Batch every 4–6 weeks, aligned with monthly dietary cube refresh.
