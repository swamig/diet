# Sunday Prep: Master Chopping & Assembly List

**Goal:** Consolidate all fresh ingredient prep into a single workflow. 
*Total Weekly Yield: 6-7 Days of Main Meals (12:00) and Salad Layers (14:30).*

---

## 1. The Garlic Station (The Allicin Pulse)
*Total Garlic needed for Sunday Prep: **Approx. 25–30 cloves***

| Task | Qty (Cloves) | Preparation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Protein Patties** | 10–14 | Smash and mince (Batch of 14 patties). **Rest 10 mins.** |
| **Bean Seasoning** | 6–7 | Crushed and rested. Fold into cold chickpeas/beans. |
| **Quinoa Sofrito** | 4–6 | Minced and rested. Use during quinoa boil. |
| **Sunday Chutneys** | 2–4 | Depending on the week's rotation (Toum or Mint/Cilantro). |

---

## 2. The Ginger & Chili Station (The TRP/Agni Pulse)
*Total needed: **Approx. 3 large Hands of Ginger** & **10–12 Chilies***

| Task | Ginger | Chilies | Preparation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Protein Patties** | 4 tbsp | 4–7 Green | Grated ginger + finely minced green chilies. |
| **Jaljeera Muddle** | 2 Thumbs | 3 Thai Bird | Sliced/crushed for the 2-part 3-day supply. |
| **Coconut Chutney** | 1/2 inch | 1 Green | Scaled 3-day batch. |
| **Quinoa/Bean Boil**| 3 inches | 2 Whole | Smashed whole ginger + whole Serranos (discard after).|

| Task | Prep Item | Amount | Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

---

### Pro-Tip: The "10-Minute Timer" Workflow
1. **First:** Peel and smash all 30 garlic cloves. Set a timer for 10 minutes.
2. **During the 10 mins:** Chop your herbs and grate your ginger.
3. **When the timer dings:** The garlic is chemically activated (Allicin formed). Now you can start mixing it into your patties, beans, and quinoa without losing the signal.
