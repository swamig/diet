# Sauce Cube Shopping — Costco Edition

Costco covers ~75% of the cube ingredients at the best bulk pricing in the system. This list maps each ingredient to its Costco SKU + pack size + approximate cost, plus a small specialty-store tail (Indian grocery / Asian market / Amazon) for the 25% that Costco doesn't reliably carry. Covers a 3-month batch of all three sauce cubes (~216 cubes total: ~96 coconut + ~96 peanut + ~24 sesame).

Companion to the generic [sauce-cube-shopping-list.md](./sauce-cube-shopping-list.md), which lists *what* you need; this list specifies *which brand at what pack size at Costco*.

---

## Costco — Aisle by Aisle

### Canned / Pantry

- ☐ **Native Forest Organic Full-Fat Coconut Milk** — 6-pack of 13.5 oz cans · need **8 cans** → buy **2 six-packs** · ~$22 each
  - Native Forest > Thai Kitchen — Native Forest has a thicker cream layer (better for the cube)
- ☐ **Kirkland Organic Peanut Butter** — 2-pack of 28 oz · need 6 cups (~3 lb) → **1 two-pack** · ~$10
  - Roasted, not raw — better freezer oxidation profile per [pad-thai-peanut-cubes.md](./pad-thai-peanut-cubes.md)
- ☐ **San-J Organic Tamari** — 64 oz · need ~3 cups → **1 bottle** · ~$14 · ~3+ batches before refill
- ☐ **Bragg's Organic ACV** — 2-pack of 32 oz · need 1 cup → **1 two-pack stockpile** · ~$10
- ☐ **Marukan Rice Vinegar** (unseasoned) — 24 oz · need ½ cup → **1 bottle** · ~$5
- ☐ **Mae Ploy Red Curry Paste** — 14 oz tub · need 1 cup → **1 tub** · ~$8
  - Not all Costcos carry this; check the Asian aisle. Fallback: build-your-own spice base from [coconut-protein-cubes.md](./coconut-protein-cubes.md).

### Produce

- ☐ **Limes** — 2 lb mesh bag (~10–12 limes) · need ~30 limes → **3 bags** · ~$15
- ☐ **Fresh ginger** — 1 lb bag · need ~12 oz → **1 bag** · ~$5
  - Freeze leftover whole per [freeze-system.md](./freeze-system.md) Sheet 12
- ☐ **Kirkland Peeled Garlic** — 24 oz tub · need ~24 cloves → **1 tub** · ~$5 · freeze leftover as rested-garlic cubes

### Dried Fruit / Sweetener

- ☐ **Kirkland Medjool Dates** — 2 lb tub (~50–60 dates) · need ~24 dates → **1 tub** · ~$10

### Spices (Kirkland brand carries most)

- ☐ **Kirkland Organic Whole Black Peppercorns** (grinder bottle) — 12 oz · crack fresh per batch · ~$8 · ~1 year shelf life
- ☐ **Kirkland Organic Turmeric** (ground) — 14 oz · need ~3 tbsp → 1 jar lasts >1 year · ~$10
- ☐ **Kirkland Smoked Paprika** — 5.5 oz · need ~3 tbsp · ~$5
- ☐ **Kirkland Sea Salt** — 30 oz · pantry staple · ~$5

### International / Asian

- ☐ **Marukome White Miso** (unpasteurized) — 35 oz tub · need ~1 cup → **1 tub** lasts 6+ months · ~$10
  - Stocked at Asian-leaning Costcos; otherwise H-Mart / Whole Foods (~$8)
- ☐ **Kadoya Toasted Sesame Oil** — large bottle if Costco carries · ~$10
  - Often only at larger Costcos; H-Mart fallback
- ☐ **Lee Kum Kee Oyster Sauce** OR skip and make [Sheet 22](./oyster-sauce-vegetarian.md) veg oyster cubes — 30 oz · 1 tbsp per batch · ~$8 (optional)

### Oils

- ☐ **Kirkland Organic Avocado Oil** — 1 L · for cube lipid lock + general use · ~$10

### Herbs (hit-or-miss · seasonal)

- ☐ **Fresh cilantro** — 3-bunch pack · ~$3
- ☐ **Fresh mint** — 4 × 4 oz clamshell pack · ~$5 · Costco rotates seasonally
- ☐ **Scallions** — 6-bunch pack · ~$3 · also seasonal

**Costco subtotal: ~$150**

## Specialty Store Tail — what Costco doesn't reliably carry

### Indian Grocery / Patel Brothers

- ☐ **Black mustard seeds** (whole) — Swad / Laxmi brand · 7 oz bag · ~$3 · 1 bag lasts a year
- ☐ **Shiitake mushroom powder** — sometimes carried; otherwise buy 1 lb dried shiitakes (~$8) and grind in Vitamix
- ☐ **Arrowroot powder** — Bob's Red Mill at Whole Foods, or Indian grocery · ~$5

### Asian Grocery / H-Mart

- ☐ **Sichuan peppercorn** (whole) — 4 oz bag · ~$5 · 1 bag lasts a year
- ☐ **Toasted sesame seeds** (light + black mix) — small jar · ~$3
- ☐ **Thai bird's eye chilies** — bunch fresh, freeze whole in labeled bag · ~$3 · 3–6 month stock per [freeze-system.md](./freeze-system.md)

### Amazon / Specialty

- ☐ **Black garlic powder** — most reliable on Amazon · ~$15 for 4 oz · lasts months

**Specialty subtotal: ~$45**

## Smart Shopping Tips

- **Two Costco runs** — round 1 for bulk perishables (coconut milk, PB, dates, limes, ginger, garlic, herbs); skip the spices on round 1 since you probably already have most of them.
- **Make the specialty trip annually** — black mustard seeds, Sichuan peppercorn, black garlic powder are all 6–12 month pantry items.
- **Frozen Thai chilies** — buy fresh at Asian grocery (cheaper than Whole Foods), wash + dry + freeze whole in a labeled bag, use direct from frozen (3–6 month stock per Sheet 12).
- **Native Forest > Thai Kitchen** for coconut milk — Native Forest has the thicker cream layer (better for the cube). Worth the ~$1/can upgrade.
- **Skip Lee Kum Kee oyster sauce if making [Sheet 22](./oyster-sauce-vegetarian.md) veg oyster cubes** — the homemade veg cubes are better quality, save shelf space.
- **Costco's Kirkland Organic spice line is excellent** — turmeric, smoked paprika, sea salt, peppercorns all reliable.

## Common Substitutions if Local Costco is Missing Items

- **Marukome miso not stocked?** → H-Mart's Hikari Organic White Miso · 14 oz tub · ~$8
- **Mae Ploy curry paste not stocked?** → use the build-your-own spice base (turmeric · cumin · coriander · cardamom · cinnamon · lemongrass paste) from [coconut-protein-cubes.md](./coconut-protein-cubes.md)
- **No Native Forest?** → Thai Kitchen Organic Full-Fat Coconut Milk · slightly watery but workable
- **Costco out of Medjool dates?** → Trader Joe's or any Middle Eastern grocery

## Storage Game Plan After Shopping

The 3-month batch shopping list creates **~$190 of inventory** that needs proper storage:

| Category | Where |
|---|---|
| Coconut milk (unopened cans) | Pantry · cool, dark |
| Peanut butter (unopened) | Pantry until first jar opens → fridge |
| Tamari, ACV, rice vinegar, sesame oil | Pantry, dark spot |
| Medjool dates | Fridge or cool pantry · ~6 mo |
| Whole spices (peppercorns, mustard seeds, Sichuan, cardamom pods) | Pantry airtight · ~1 yr |
| Fresh ginger | **Freeze whole** per Sheet 12 |
| Garlic | **Rested garlic cubes** per Sheet 12 |
| Fresh herbs | Fridge in damp paper towel · rotate weekly |
| Thai chilies | Freeze whole in labeled bag |
| Miso | **Fridge always · do NOT freeze** (kills LAB) |
