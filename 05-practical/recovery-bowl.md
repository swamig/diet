# Recovery Bowl — 17:50 Stack

The complete 17:50 recovery window. Two components consumed together:
1. **Yogurt Recovery Bowl** — casein anchor + anthocyanins + phantom sweetness
2. **Aambali** — fermented ragi probiotic drink (see [Protocol Aambali](protocol-aambali.md))

Together: RS3 + live probiotics (aambali) + slow-release casein + quercetin + anthocyanins (bowl).

---

## 1. Base Bowl

| Ingredient | Amount | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Full-fat Greek yogurt | 200g (~¾ cup) | Casein anchor + calcium shield (~200mg Ca) |
| Blueberries | ¾ cup | Anthocyanin + polyphenol pulse |
| Casein protein (or pea + rice protein) | 1 scoop | Slow-release MPS overnight |
| Black bean liquid (optional) | 2 tbsp frozen cube | Cyanidin-3-glucoside boost |
| Black garlic | 2–3 cloves, sliced | SAC — sustained cardiovascular protection |

---

## 2. Phantom Sweetness Stack

Trick the brain into sweetness perception without an insulin spike. No sugar, no stevia.

| Ingredient | Amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Ceylon Cinnamon | pinch (~⅛ tsp) | Glycemic anchor + olfactory sweetness signal |
| Cardamom (ground) | pinch | Parasympathetic calm, neuro-modulator |
| Nutmeg (fresh-grated preferred) | pinch | Warming aromatic depth |
| **Pure Madagascar Vanilla Extract** | **¼ tsp — CACAO DAYS ONLY** | Anti-mutagenic vanillin. Paired with cacao. NOT a daily baseline — prevents receptor downregulation. |

---

## 3. Cacao Rules (2–3× per week MAX, never daily)

- Amount: ½–1 tsp raw cacao nibs stirred in
- **Calcium shield is mandatory**: Greek yogurt must be in the bowl (~200mg Ca binds cacao oxalates in the gut before the kidneys see them)
- **Vanilla is only added on cacao days** — not as a daily add-on
- **Chocolate whey day**: skip raw cacao nibs — that already counts as one cacao exposure
- Reference: [Oxalate Management](../04-advanced-control/oxalate-management.md), [Oncology Chemoprevention](../04-advanced-control/oncology-chemoprevention.md)

## 4. Day-Type Rules

| Day Type | Casein | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Training day | 1 scoop | Full bowl |
| Rest / Cardio day | **SKIP casein scoop** | Yogurt + berries + phantom sweetness only |
| Missed 17:30 window | + 1 scoop Unflavored Whey | Add alongside casein |

---

## 5. Aambali Companion Drink

See full protocol: [Protocol Aambali](protocol-aambali.md)

**Quick reference:**
- **07:00 prep:** Scoop ½ cup chilled ragi paste, whisk with ¼ cup live yogurt + splash water → buttermilk consistency. Leave covered 10–12h.
- **Sunday batch cook:** 1 cup ragi flour + 3–4 cups cold water → cook to thick paste → refrigerate (lasts 5 days). Scoop daily.
- **Finish at 17:50:** Add ½ tsp cumin powder + pinch black salt + 25g pea/rice protein. Froth. Add 5–10 fresh peppermint leaves (muddled).
- **No-heat rule:** NO chili, ginger, habanero — this window requires core-temp drop for sleep.
- **Starch split rule:** If taking Aambali → reduce 12:00 quinoa/starch by 50%. Aambali IS the evening starch anchor.

---

## 6. Savory Chaas Variant (replaces sweet bowl)

Use on: hot weather, digestive rest days, when sweet bowl feels heavy.

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Greek yogurt | 100g |
| Cold water | ½ cup |
| Roasted cumin powder | ¼ tsp |
| Black salt (kala namak) | pinch |
| Unflavored protein | 1 scoop |

> **MUST NOT be spicy.** No chili, no hing, no pepper. No exceptions.

Blend until smooth. The tangy + cumin profile satisfies the recovery signal without heaviness.

---

## 7. Black Garlic (SAC)

Slice 2–3 cloves directly into the yogurt bowl (or mince into chaas).

Black garlic provides **S-allyl cysteine (SAC)** — heat-stable, long half-life, sustained cardiovascular protection via H₂S donor activity. Distinct from allicin (acute, enzymatic, short half-life from rested raw garlic). No redundancy — run both.

**Making black garlic:** whole garlic heads in rice cooker on Keep Warm (140–170°F) for 3–4 weeks.
Reference: [Master Batch Prep — Black Garlic](master-batch-prep.md#black-garlic-passive-fermentation--start-on-3-month-day)
