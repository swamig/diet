# Blueprint Product Integration (Bryan Johnson)

This manual is built on a **food-first, mechanism-driven** architecture. Bryan Johnson’s *Blueprint* protocol is a highly optimized, **supplement-heavy** longevity system. 

If you are using or transitioning to Blueprint products (Nutty Pudding, Super Veggie, Green Giant, Snake Oil), you cannot simply layer them on top of this manual's existing meals. Doing so will violate the Oxalate Management, Sulforaphane Activation, and Liver Load rules. 

Here is exactly how to integrate Blueprint elements into this protocol's biological windows.

## 1. Extra Virgin Olive Oil ("Snake Oil")
*   **The Fit:** Perfect integration. 
*   **The Execution:** Use Blueprint EVOO (high polyphenol content) exclusively for your **7-Day Dressing Master Batch** and to bloom your spices/chilies during the **Tofu Day High Pulse**. The high polyphenol count synergizes perfectly with the capsaicin and curcumin absorption strategies.

## 2. Super Veggie
*   **The Conflict:** Blueprint's Super Veggie boils/steams the broccoli. In our manual, this destroys the *myrosinase* enzyme and prevents Sulforaphane formation. It also lacks the specific Turmeric/Black Pepper ratios required by our inflammatory control baseline.
*   **The Integration:** Use Super Veggie as a direct replacement for your **12:00 Main Meal** (instead of Vegan Patties or Tofu).
*   **The Protocol Fix:** 
    1.  You **must** add 1/2 tsp of Mustard Seed Powder to the Super Veggie *after* it is cooked and removed from the heat. This provides exogenous myrosinase and restores the sulforaphane reaction.
    2.  Add the protocol's mandatory 1/2 tsp Turmeric and 1/4 tsp Black Pepper.

## 3. Nutty Pudding
*   **The Conflict:** Nutty Pudding is incredibly dense in oxalates (berries, macadamia nuts, walnuts, chia). If you eat this *and* the protocol's standard meals, you will exceed your kidneys' clearance capacity.
*   **The Integration:** Use Nutty Pudding as a direct replacement for the **17:50 Recovery Bowl**.
*   **The Protocol Fix:** Because Nutty Pudding does not contain Greek Yogurt (calcium), you must ensure you have a calcium source nearby to bind the oxalates in the gut. Consider adding a scoop of Casein or consuming alongside your Whey Isolate.

## 4. Green Giant
*   **The Conflict:** Green Giant contains Cocoa Flavanols. Our protocol strictly limits and spaces cacao exposure due to its heavy metal (cadmium/lead) and oxalate footprint. 
*   **The Integration:** Drink Green Giant during the **14:30 Salad Layer**. 
*   **The Protocol Fix:** On days you drink Green Giant, you are on a **"Cacao Day."** You must strictly skip any raw cacao nibs in your evening recovery bowl. Do not stack them.

## 5. Longevity Supplements (NMN, NR, Spermidine)
*   **The Conflict:** Taking a massive handful of isolated molecules on an empty stomach triggers rapid Phase I liver detoxification, which can cause nausea and flush the compounds before they are utilized.
*   **The Integration:** Take the Blueprint Longevity stack strictly at **12:00** alongside your Omega-3s, Vitamin D, and Main Meal. The fats and fiber in the meal slow gastric emptying, providing a steady, manageable absorption curve for the liver.
