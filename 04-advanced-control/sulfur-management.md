# Sulfur Cycle Optimization

Sulfur-containing compounds influence glutathione synthesis.

## Support Mechanisms in This Protocol:

1.  **Cruciferous vegetables** (sulforaphane)
2.  **Garlic and onion family** inclusion (when layered)
3.  **Adequate protein** intake

Week 2 emphasizes sulfur pathway support.
Sulfur intake is balanced to avoid gastrointestinal distress.

## Evidence Status

- **General physiology:** The interaction and load-management ideas on this page are used as conservative operating guidance inside this protocol.
