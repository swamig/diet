# Antimicrobial Stacking Framework

Spices like Clove and Ajwain are potent antimicrobial agents.

**Excessive daily stacking can disrupt microbiome diversity.**

## Stacking Rules

1.  **Clove** is not stacked with **Ajwain** on the same day.
2.  **Nigella** (Black Jeera) is limited to controlled frequency.
3.  **Fermented Vegetables** are consumed daily (2 tbsp) to maintain microbial diversity.
4.  **Clove Water** is limited to 1-2 times per week max.
