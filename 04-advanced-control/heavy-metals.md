# Heavy Metal Mitigation (Cacao)

Cacao is a potent signaling compound but accumulates heavy metals (Cadmium, Lead).

## Mitigation Strategy

1.  **Sourcing:** Prioritize brands that test for heavy metals (e.g., Navitas, specific boutique producers).
2.  **Rotation:** Do not consume daily. Limit to 2–3x per week.
3.  **Dose Control:** 1 tablespoon (approx 10g) is the therapeutic window.
4.  **Fiber Pairing:** Consumed with fiber (blueberries) to reduce absorption.

- **General physiology:** The interaction and load-management ideas on this page are used as conservative operating guidance inside this protocol.
