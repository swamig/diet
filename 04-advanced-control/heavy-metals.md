# Heavy Metal Mitigation

Environmental heavy metal exposure (Cadmium, Lead, Arsenic) is a significant metabolic stressor. This protocol uses a tiered mitigation strategy focusing on extraction and excretion.

## 1. Produce Mitigation (The Baking Soda Soak)
*   **The Mechanism:** Most heavy metals and organophosphate pesticides are surface-level contaminants. 
*   **The Action:** All produce (Greens, Microgreens, Herbs) MUST undergo a 12-minute **[Baking Soda Soak](../05-practical/produce-cleaning.md)** to neutralize alkaline-sensitive toxins before consumption.

## 2. Enteric Binding (The Imlee Hack)
*   **The Mechanism:** Tamarind pulp is rich in pectin and mucilage which act as "enteric sweeps."
*   **The Action:** Consuming the **[Imlee Chutney](../05-practical/functional-indian-formats.md#1-the-enteric-binder-imlee--tamarind-chutney)** daily helps trap heavy metal particulates in the GI tract, preventing absorption into the bloodstream.

## 3. Chelation Anchor (Cilantro)
*   **The Mechanism:** Cilantro is documented as a natural chelator that assists in mobilizing heavy metals from deep tissues for excretion [[10]](../07-appendices/master-citations.md#10-antioxidants-polyphenols--vascular-signaling).
*   **The Action:** 1/4 to 1/2 cup of fresh **[Cilantro](../02-ingredients/cilantro.md)** is a mandatory component of the 14:30 Salad Layer.

## 4. Specific Compound Management (Cacao)
Cacao is a potent signaling compound but naturally accumulates Cadmium and Lead from volcanic soil.

*   **Sourcing:** Prioritize brands that test for heavy metals (e.g., Navitas, specific boutique producers).
*   **Rotation:** Do not consume daily. Limit to 2–3x per week as defined in **[Supplement Cycling](supplement-cycling.md)**.
*   **Fiber Pairing:** Always consume with fiber (e.g., **[Blueberries](../03-execution/week-01/day-01.md#recovery-bowl)**) to further inhibit intestinal absorption of heavy metals.
