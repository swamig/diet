# Metabolic Oncology & Chemoprevention

This protocol is engineered as a comprehensive chemopreventive system. While no diet can guarantee the prevention of cancer, this manual specifically attacks the four "Hallmarks of Cancer" (tumor promotion, genomic instability, immune evasion, and sustained proliferative signaling) through dietary biochemistry and autonomic nervous system management.

## 1. Nrf2 Activation (The Sulforaphane Engine)
*   **The Mechanism:** Cancer initiation often begins with Reactive Oxygen Species (ROS) causing DNA damage. Sulforaphane is the most potent naturally occurring inducer of the Nrf2 pathway.
*   **The Protocol:** Nrf2 acts as a cellular "thermostat" for oxidative stress. When activated, it translocates to the nucleus and turns on over 200 antioxidant and Phase II detoxification genes. 
*   **Execution:** The non-negotiable **"Chop-and-Rest Rule"** (30 minutes) for broccoli ensures the enzyme *myrosinase* converts glucoraphanin into bioavailable sulforaphane.

## 2. NF-κB Inhibition (The Inflammatory Blockade)
*   **The Mechanism:** Chronic inflammation is the "paving stone" for cancer, providing the cytokines necessary for tumor angiogenesis (blood vessel growth) and metastasis.
*   **The Protocol:** Nuclear factor-kappa B (NF-κB) is the master transcription factor for inflammation. The protocol anchors the day with three extreme inhibitors of this pathway:
    *   **Curcumin (Turmeric)** + Piperine (Black Pepper)
    *   **6-Gingerol (Fresh Ginger)**: The "Daily Thumb" mandate.
    *   **Tocotrienols (Achiote/Annatto)**

## 3. IGF-1 Suppression (The 18:6 Feeding Window)
*   **The Mechanism:** Insulin-like Growth Factor 1 (IGF-1) is a primary proliferative signal for many cancer cell lines (breast, prostate, colon). 
*   **The Protocol:** The manual uses an 18-hour fasting window (18:00 to 12:00) to keep insulin and IGF-1 suppressed. 
*   **Autophagy:** Fasting beyond 16 hours also triggers macroautophagy—the cellular cleanup process where lysosomes degrade misfolded proteins and damaged organelles before they can mutate into oncogenes.

## 4. Psychoneuroimmunology (Stress & Immune Surveillance)
*   **The Mechanism (Cortisol Immune-Suppression):** The immune system relies on Natural Killer (NK) cells and Cytotoxic T lymphocytes to identify and destroy nascent cancer cells. Chronic psychological stress causes a sustained release of cortisol, which directly paralyzes these immune cells, allowing tumors to evade detection (immune evasion).
*   **The Protocol:** You cannot out-supplement a chronically stressed mind. The manual deploys:
    *   **Restorative Yoga (Vagal Tone):** Stimulating the Vagus nerve to drop cortisol mechanically.
    *   **Shaiva Tantra / Mantra:** Using cognitive anchors to silence the Default Mode Network (DMN), preventing the rumination that keeps the HPA axis engaged.
    *   **Adaptogen Cycling (Ashwagandha):** Using the 21:00 Night Infusion to chemically buffer cortisol and enforce a deep parasympathetic state.

## 5. Endocrine Disruption (The Hardware Mandate)
*   **The Mechanism:** Chemical plasticizers (BPA, BPS, Phthalates) and "forever chemicals" (PFOA/PFAS) found in non-stick pans and plastic Tupperware act as xenoestrogens. They bind to hormone receptors, promoting estrogen-driven cancers and disrupting the androgenic signaling required for physical recovery.
*   **The Protocol:** The manual strictly enforces a **100% Glass Storage** and **Stainless/Ceramic Cookware** rule. 
*   **Pesticide Mitigation:** The 12-minute **Baking Soda Soak** removes organophosphates from produce, further reducing the chemical load on the liver.
