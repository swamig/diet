# Ayurvedic Alignment & Doshic Balance

While this manual is primarily organized around modern biochemical signaling (AMPK, Nrf2, TRP), its operational logic is deeply aligned with Ayurvedic principles. This page provides the traditional framework for the protocol’s "Heating" and "Cooling" synergies.

## 1. Agni (Digestive Fire) vs. Systemic pH

The protocol rejects the "Alkaline Diet" trend in favor of kindling **Agni** (digestive fire).

*   **The Logic:** Western "alkalinity" often leads to raw, cold, and bland foods that "extinguish" digestive fire, leading to *Ama* (metabolic sludge/toxins).
*   **The Protocol Fix:** We use **Amla Rasa** (Sour/Acidic taste) from lemon juice and vinegar to kindle Agni. This low-pH environment ensures heavy plant proteins and starches are fully "transformed" rather than fermented in the gut.

## 2. TRP Synergy (Pitta/Sheeta Balance)

The **Menthol + Capsaicin** stack is a modern version of Doshic balancing.

*   **Pitta (Heating):** Chilies and Garlic are intensely *Ushna* (Heating). Used alone, they can aggravate *Pitta*, leading to systemic "burn" (inflammation).
*   **Sheeta (Cooling):** Mentha arvensis and Cilantro are intensely *Sheeta* (Cooling). 
*   **The Synergy:** Consuming them together in the **Peppermint Jaljeera** creates a balanced signal. You get the metabolic stimulation of the heat (Capsaicin) without the inflammatory side effects, maintaining a state of **Sama** (equilibrium).

## 3. Shodhana (Circadian Cleansing)

The morning hydration protocol (07:15) functions as a daily **Shodhana** (purification) ritual.

*   **Ajwain Water:** Acts as an anti-spasmodic to clear the channels (*Srotas*) of gas.
*   **Jeera Water:** Cools the system and prepares the "soil" (gut lining) for the nutrient-dense feeding window at 12:00.

## 4. Tamasic Management (Garlic Logic)

In Ayurveda, Garlic is considered *Tamasic* (heavy/grounding). 

*   **The 10-Minute Rest Rule:** This ensures the "medicinal" (Sattvic) potential of the garlic—the Allicin—is fully activated before it hit the heat.
*   **The Timing:** Timing the "High Pulse" Garlic/Chili oil to 12:00 aligns with the peak of the sun, when the human Agni is strongest and most capable of processing heavy organosulfur compounds.
