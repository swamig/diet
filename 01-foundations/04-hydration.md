# Circadian Hydration Physiology

Hydration in this protocol is not neutral. It is divided into:

## Daytime Hydration (Sympathetic Alignment)

*   **Ajwain**
*   **Jeera (Cumin)**
*   **Ginger**
*   **Clove (limited)**
*   **Mint**

These support digestion, circulation, and metabolic readiness.

## Night Hydration (Parasympathetic Alignment)

*   **Saffron**
*   **Tulsi (Holy Basil)**
*   **Cinnamon**
*   **Warm mineral water**

These support nervous system downregulation, glucose stability, and recovery.

Hydration timing is therefore a signaling tool, not merely fluid replacement.

## Conflicts

- Letting structured hydration disappear usually weakens adherence to the rest of the day rhythm.
- Replacing the evening downshift infusion with a later stimulant works against the recovery cue used in this manual.
