# Sesame Seed - Mineral Density Layer

## Active Compounds

*   **Sesamin**
*   **Sesamol**
*   **Calcium**
*   **Magnesium**

## Mechanism of Action

Sesame:

*   **Provides mineral density**
*   **Influences lipid metabolism**
*   **Supports antioxidant status**

## Dose

*   1 tablespoon (10–15g)
*   **Rotated, not daily.**

## Performance Application

*   **On non-cacao days**
*   **When magnesium density is desired**
*   **Not stacked with chia on same day**
