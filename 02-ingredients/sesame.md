# Sesame Seed - Mineral Density Layer

## Active Compounds

*   **Sesamin**
*   **Sesamol**
*   **Calcium**
*   **Magnesium**

## Mechanism of Action

Sesame [[Adlercreutz, 2007]](../../07-appendices/master-citations.md#10-antioxidants-polyphenols--vascular-signaling):

*   **Provides mineral density**
*   **Influences lipid metabolism** via sesamin and sesamol lignans
*   **Modulates SHBG:** Plant lignans (sesamin/sesamol) stimulate hepatic synthesis of Sex Hormone-Binding Globulin, buffering rapid spikes and dips in circulating sex hormones
*   **Supports antioxidant status**

## Dose

*   1 tablespoon (10–15g)
*   **Rotated, not daily.**

## Protocol Role

*   **On non-cacao days**
*   **When magnesium density is desired**
*   **Not stacked with chia on same day**

## Stacking Rules

- Use sesame as part of the salad’s mineral and texture layer rather than as an isolated snack-like add-on.
- Rotate it with other seeds when the protocol calls for variety, but keep the role consistent.
