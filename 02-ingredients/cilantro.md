# Cilantro - Heavy Metal Chelation and Antioxidant Support

## Active Compounds

*   **Linalool**
*   **Quercetin**
*   **Dodecenal** (antimicrobial)

## Mechanism of Action

Cilantro:

*   **Mobilizes heavy metals** (mild chelation effect)
*   **Supports liver detoxification**
*   **Reduces oxidative stress**
*   **Enhances digestion**

## Dose

*   **Generous handful daily (1/4 - 1/2 cup chopped)**.

## Stacking Rules

*   **Included in salads or as garnish daily.**
*   **Pairs well with microgreens.**
*   **Do not cook heavily; add fresh.**

## Protocol Role

- Cilantro is a daily fresh-herb anchor that keeps the salad layer feeling alive and consistent.
- It is used for freshness, garnish density, and the manual’s broader heavy-metal and herb-layer logic.

- **Protocol-specific rules:** The exact dose, timing, pairings, weekly caps, and "mandatory" language on this page are operating rules specific to this manual.
