# Fermented Vegetables - Microbiome Stabilization Layer

## Mechanism of Action

*   **Introduce beneficial microbes**
*   **Produce short-chain fatty acids**
*   **Enhance gut barrier integrity**

## Dose

*   **2 tablespoons daily.**

## Protocol Role

Daily low-dose exposure supports:

*   **Immune modulation**
*   **Recovery**
*   **Fiber fermentation efficiency**

Not stacked with heavy antimicrobial days.

## Stacking Rules

- Keep ferments in the daily small-dose rhythm instead of saving them for occasional larger servings.
- Use them to stabilize antimicrobial-heavy weeks rather than removing them.

## Evidence Status

- **General physiology:** The compound and mechanism descriptions on this page are used as directional context for how the ingredient may fit into the protocol.
- **Protocol-specific rules:** The exact dose, timing, pairings, weekly caps, and "mandatory" language on this page are operating rules specific to this manual.
