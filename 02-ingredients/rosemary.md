# Rosemary - Cognitive and Lipid Protection Agent

## Active Compounds

*   Carnosic acid
*   Rosmarinic acid

## Mechanism of Action

Rosemary:

*   Supports **lipid oxidation stability**
*   **Protects neuronal tissue**
*   Modulates oxidative stress

## Dose

*   1 tsp chopped
*   **Used 2-3x per week as garnish**

## Protocol Role

- Rosemary is a selective support herb used on days where the manual wants a sharper cognitive or vascular-support feel.
- It is a rotator, not a required daily layer.

## Stacking Rules

- Use rosemary selectively on the days that call for it instead of adding it everywhere by default.
- Keep it as a rotator inside the broader herb system.
