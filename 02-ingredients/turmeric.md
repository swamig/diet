# Turmeric (Foundational Anti-Inflammatory Anchor)

## Active Compounds

*   **Curcumin**
*   Demethoxycurcumin
*   Bisdemethoxycurcumin
*   Volatile oils

## Mechanism of Action

Curcumin modulates multiple cellular pathways:

*   Downregulates **NF-κB** transcription activity
*   Suppresses **COX-2** enzyme expression
*   Reduces **TNF-α** signaling
*   Influences **Nrf2** antioxidant pathway
*   Modulates **AMPK** signaling

## Dose in This Protocol

*   **Daily:** 1/2 teaspoon (~1.5 grams)
*   **Paired with:** 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

## Bioavailability Strategy

Curcumin is poorly absorbed in isolation. **Piperine** (from black pepper) increases bioavailability by inhibiting hepatic glucuronidation. This pairing is mandatory.

## Stacking Rules

Turmeric is:

*   Included daily
*   Anchors the stack
*   Compatible with all other spices
