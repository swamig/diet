# Ajwain (Carom Seed) - Digestive Activation Agent

## Active Compounds

*   **Thymol**

## Mechanism of Action

Ajwain:

*   **Stimulates digestive enzymes**
*   **Reduces gas formation**
*   **Mild antimicrobial modulation**
*   **Enhances gastric motility**

## Dose

*   1/4 teaspoon steeped in hot water.
*   **2–3x weekly.**

## Stacking Rules

*   **Not stacked with clove.**
*   **Not stacked with heavy antimicrobial days.**
*   **Used primarily in morning hydration.**

## Protocol Role

- Ajwain functions as a digestive activation and antimicrobial pulse ingredient inside the protocol.
- It shows up in hydration and spice-support contexts when the day needs stronger digestive signaling.
