# Cumin (Jeera) - Digestive and Iron Support Layer

## Active Compounds

*   Cuminaldehyde
*   Terpenes
*   Flavonoids

## Mechanism of Action

Cumin supports:

*   **Digestive enzyme secretion**
*   **Mild antimicrobial modulation**
*   **Iron absorption**
*   **Glucose regulation**

Cumin is included daily at moderate dose to enhance digestive efficiency.

## Dose

*   1/2 teaspoon (~1 gram)

## Stacking Rules

Cumin:

*   Safe for daily inclusion
*   Compatible with turmeric
*   Does not require spacing
