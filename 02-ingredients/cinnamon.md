# Cinnamon (Ceylon) - Glucose Modulation Agent

## Active Compound

*   **Cinnamaldehyde**

## Mechanism of Action

Cinnamon influences:

*   **Insulin sensitivity**
*   **Glucose transport**
*   **Postprandial glucose modulation**

**Ceylon cinnamon** is used to avoid excessive coumarin exposure.

## Dose

*   1/4 teaspoon in warm water at night.
*   **2–3x weekly.**

## Performance Application

*   **On higher carbohydrate days**
*   **During Week 3 nitric oxide emphasis**
*   **On cacao stack days to moderate glucose load**
