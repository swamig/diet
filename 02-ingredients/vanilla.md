# Pure Vanilla Extract (Vanillin / Neuro-Calmer)

Pure Madagascar Vanilla Extract is utilized in this protocol as a **"Phantom Sweetness"** agent and a potent **anti-mutagenic** signaling tool.

## Mechanism of Action (Vanillin)

*   **Olfactory Sweetness (Phantom Sweetness):** Vanillin provides a deep, sweet aroma that tricks the brain into perceiving sweetness without the presence of glucose. This allows for the stabilization of the post-meal insulin curve while satisfying the palate's drive for a "dessert" finish.
*   **Neuro-Modulation:** The scent of vanilla has been documented to induce a parasympathetic state, lowering heart rate and promoting a "rest and digest" signal in the nervous system.
*   **Anti-Mutagenic Signaling:** Vanillin is a potent free radical scavenger. When consumed alongside cacao flavanols, it acts synergistically to protect DNA from oxidative stress.

## Execution

*   **Timing:** 17:50 (Recovery Bowl) and 21:00 (Night Infusion - Ashwagandha variant).
*   **Dose:** 1/4 tsp Pure Madagascar Vanilla Extract.
*   **Protocol Rule (Pulsing):** To prevent receptor down-regulation and biological tolerance, Vanilla Extract is explicitly "pulsed" **2-3x per week**. It is typically added to the Recovery Bowl on the same days Cacao Nibs are included.
