# Saffron - Neuro-Modulatory Microdose

## Active Compounds

*   **Crocin**
*   **Safranal**

## Mechanism of Action

Saffron influences:

*   **Mood regulation**
*   **Neuroinflammation**
*   **Mitochondrial signaling**
*   **Sleep architecture**

## Dose

*   5 threads.
*   **Microdose only.**
*   **Used 2-3x per week max.**
*   **Steep in warm water for 10-15 min.**

## Protocol Role

Saffron is used as a parasympathetic signal pulse, not a background layer. Hormesis works best when pulsed.

## Stacking Rules

- Keep saffron in the 2-3x weekly microdose range used by the evening downshift protocol.
- Use it as a pulse, not a continuous background ingredient.
