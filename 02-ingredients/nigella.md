# Nigella (Black Jeera) - Metabolic Modulation Agent

## Active Compound

*   **Thymoquinone**

## Mechanism of Action

Thymoquinone influences:

*   **Glucose regulation**
*   **Inflammatory modulation**
*   **Mitochondrial protection**
*   **Mild antimicrobial effects**

Nigella is more pharmacologically active than cumin. Therefore it is not daily.

## Dose

*   1/4 teaspoon (~0.7 grams)
*   **Used 3x per week.**

## Stacking Restrictions

Nigella is:

*   **Not stacked with high clove days**
*   **Not stacked with excessive antimicrobial load**
*   Rotated to prevent microbiome suppression
