## Hydration
**Morning: Ginger + Lemon**
*   4 slices ginger
*   Steep 10 min, add lemon.

**Night: Saffron Infusion**
*   5 saffron threads
*   Warm water (80-90°C)
*   Steep 12-15 min.

## Main Meal: Vegan Patties Baseline
**Ingredients:**
*   2 Vegan Protein Patties + 1/2 cup Chickpeas
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min)
*   1/2 cup quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **No nigella. No clove.**

**Instructions:**
1.  Sear patties with spice mix.
2.  Sear 5-6 min/side (165°F).
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.

## Salad: Peak Nitric Layer

*   2 cups watercress

*   **3/4 cup microgreens** (Daily Essential)

*   2 tbsp green onions
*   **1/2 cup pomegranate seeds**
*   Dressing: Olive oil + Balsamic + 1 tbsp Tomato Puree + 1 tsp Dijon Mustard + Pinch Turmeric + Pinch Black Pepper
