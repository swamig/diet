## Hydration
**Morning: Ajwain Water**
*   1/4 tsp ajwain
*   Simmer 2 min, steep 10 min.

**Night: Ashwagandha (Tasty Net 8)**
*   1/2 tsp Ashwagandha root powder
*   1/4 tsp Ceylon Cinnamon + pinch Cardamom
*   1 cup warm Alkaline Water (pH 8+) + splash almond milk.

## Main Meal: Tofu + Nigella
**Ingredients:**
*   200g tofu (pressed 15 min)
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min [4](../../07-appendices/master-citations.md#4-sulforaphane--inflammatory-control))
*   1/2 cup quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **1/4 tsp nigella** (Mitochondrial support)

**Instructions:**
1.  Rub tofu with spice mix/nigella.
2.  Sear 3-4 min/side.
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.

## Salad
