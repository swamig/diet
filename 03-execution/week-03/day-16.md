Mitochondrial modulation + sulfur continuation.

## Hydration
**Morning: Ajwain Water**
*   1/4 tsp ajwain
*   Simmer 2 min, steep 10 min.

**Night: Tulsi Infusion**
*   1 tsp dried tulsi
*   Steep 7 min.

## Main Meal: Tofu + Nigella
**Ingredients:**
*   200g tofu (pressed 15 min)
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min)
*   1/2 cup quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **1/4 tsp nigella** (Mitochondrial support)

**Instructions:**
1.  Rub tofu with spice mix/nigella.
2.  Sear 3-4 min/side.
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.

## Salad
**Ingredients:**
*   2 cups kale (massaged 60 sec)
*   2 tbsp sauerkraut
*   1 tbsp walnuts
*   **1/2 cup microgreens**
*   **Generous handful fresh cilantro**
*   2 tbsp green onions
*   Dressing: Olive oil + Balsamic + 1 tbsp Tomato Puree + 1 tsp Dijon Mustard + Pinch Turmeric + Pinch Black Pepper

**Note:** No pomegranate today.

## Post-Workout Protocol

## Recovery Bowl

*   Casein
*   Blueberries
*   **No cacao (spacing oxalate).**

## Clinical Rationale
*   Nigella provides mitochondrial and glucose modulation.
*   Tulsi supports adaptogenic response.
