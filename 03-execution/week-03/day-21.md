## Objective
Parasympathetic emphasis + low stimulation reset.

## Timing
*   **06:30** – Fasted cardio
*   **07:15** – Jeera hydration
*   **12:00** – Break fast
*   **14:30** – Salad
*   **16:30** – Resistance
*   **17:30** – Protein
*   **17:50** – Recovery
*   **21:00** – Saffron infusion

## Hydration
**Morning: Jeera Water**
*   1/2 tsp cumin seeds
*   Boil 5 min.

**Night: Saffron Infusion**
*   5 saffron threads
*   Warm water (80-90°C)
*   Steep 12-15 min.

## Main Meal: Vegan Patties Baseline (Low Spice Load)
**Ingredients:**
*   2 Vegan Protein Patties + 1/2 cup Chickpeas
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min)
*   1/2 cup quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **No nigella. No clove. No rosemary.**

**Instructions:**
1.  Sear patties with spice mix.
2.  Sear 5-6 min/side (165°F).
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.

## Salad: Reset Stack
**Ingredients:**
*   2 cups kale
*   2 tbsp sauerkraut
*   1 tbsp sesame
*   **1/2 cup microgreens**
*   **Generous handful fresh cilantro**
*   2 tbsp green onions
*   Dressing: Olive oil + Balsamic + 1 tbsp Tomato Puree + 1 tsp Dijon Mustard + Pinch Turmeric + Pinch Black Pepper

**Note:** No pomegranate, walnuts, or pumpkin.

## Post-Workout Protocol

- Standard 17:30 whey isolate + creatine window unless this day explicitly changes it.

## Recovery Bowl
*   Greek yogurt
*   Casein
*   Blueberries
*   **No cacao.**

## Clinical Rationale
*   Reduced stimulation allowing nervous system reset before Week 4.
*   Saffron used for mood stabilization.
