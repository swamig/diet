## Main Meal: Tofu Baseline (Low Spice Load)
**Ingredients:**
*   200g firm tofu (pressed 15 min)
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min)
*   90g cooked quinoa
*   5g olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **No nigella, no clove.**

**Instructions:**
1.  Rub tofu with spice mix.
2.  Sear 3-4 min/side.
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.

## Salad: Reset Stack
**Ingredients:**
*   2 cups arugula
*   2 tbsp sauerkraut
*   1 tbsp sesame
*   **1/2 cup microgreens**
*   **Generous handful fresh cilantro**
*   2 tbsp green onions
*   Dressing: Olive oil + Balsamic + 1 tbsp Tomato Puree + 1 tsp Dijon Mustard + Pinch Turmeric + Pinch Black Pepper

**Note:** No pomegranate, walnuts, or pumpkin.

## Clinical Rationale
