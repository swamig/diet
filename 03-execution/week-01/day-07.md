## Hydration
**Morning: Saffron Infusion**
*   5 saffron threads
*   Warm water (80-90°C)
*   Steep 10-15 min.

**Night: Tulsi Infusion**
*   1 tsp dried tulsi
*   Warm water (pH 8+ Optional)
*   Steep 10 mins. Drink warm 60 min before sleep.
