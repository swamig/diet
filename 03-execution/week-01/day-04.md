Fiber modulation + legume exposure.

*   **16:30** – Resistance

## Hydration
**Morning: Clove Infusion**
*   1 whole clove
*   Simmer 2 min, steep 8 min. Remove clove.

**Night: Mineral Water**
*   Warm water + pinch sea salt.

## Main Meal: Lentil Protein Rotation
**Ingredients:**
*   1 cup cooked lentils (approx 200g)
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min)
*   1/2 cup cooked quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **No nigella. No clove.**

**Instructions:**
1.  Warm lentils gently (pre-cooked).
2.  Add spices after warming to preserve volatile compounds.
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.

## Salad
**Ingredients:**
*   2 cups romaine
*   2 tbsp sauerkraut
*   1 tbsp sesame
*   **1/2 cup microgreens**
*   **Generous handful fresh cilantro**
*   2 tbsp green onions
*   Dressing: Olive oil + Balsamic + 1 tbsp Tomato Puree + 1 tsp Dijon Mustard + Pinch Turmeric + Pinch Black Pepper

**Note:** No pomegranate today.

## Post-Workout Protocol

## Recovery Bowl

*   **No cacao.**

## Clinical Rationale

*   SCFA production emphasis
*   Clove used as periodic antimicrobial reset
