## Main Meal: Lentil Protein Rotation
**Ingredients:**
*   1 cup cooked lentils (approx 200g)
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min)
*   1/2 cup cooked quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **No nigella. No clove.**

**Instructions:**
1.  Warm lentils gently (pre-cooked).
2.  Add spices after warming to preserve volatile compounds.
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.
