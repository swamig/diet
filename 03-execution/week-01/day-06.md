## Hydration
**Morning: Mineral Salt Water**
*   240 ml warm water
*   Small pinch unrefined sea salt (~0.3g)

**Night: Saffron Infusion**
*   5 saffron threads
*   Warm Alkaline Water (pH 8+) ONLY. (No masking).
*   Steep 10-15 min. Drink 60 min before sleep.

## Main Meal: Vegan Patties + Nigella Stack
**Ingredients:**
*   2 Vegan Protein Patties + 1/2 cup Chickpeas
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min)
*   90g cooked quinoa
*   5g olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **1/4 tsp nigella** (Moderate antimicrobial)

**Instructions:**
1.  Sear patties with spice mix/nigella.
2.  Sear 5-6 min/side (165°F).
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.

## Salad: Walnut Stack
**Ingredients:**
*   2 cups watercress
*   2 tbsp sauerkraut
*   1 tbsp walnuts
*   **1/2 cup microgreens**
*   **Generous handful fresh cilantro**
*   2 tbsp green onions
*   Dressing: Olive oil + Balsamic + 1 tbsp Tomato Puree + 1 tsp Dijon Mustard + Pinch Turmeric + Pinch Black Pepper

**Note:** No pomegranate today.
