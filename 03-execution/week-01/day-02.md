## Main Meal: Tofu Anti-Inflammatory Protocol
**Ingredients:**
*   200g firm tofu (pressed 15 min)
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min)
*   90g cooked quinoa
*   5g olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   1/4 tsp nigella (First moderate antimicrobial)

**Instructions:**
1.  Rub tofu with spice mix/nigella.
2.  Sear 3-4 min/side.
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.
