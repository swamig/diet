## Hydration
**Morning: Jeera Water**
*   1/2 tsp cumin seeds
*   Boil 5 min.

**Night: Tulsi Infusion**
*   1 tsp dried tulsi
*   Warm water (pH 8+ Optional)
*   Steep 10 mins. Drink warm 60 min before sleep.

## Main Meal: Vegan Patties [8](../../07-appendices/master-citations.md#8-protein-architecture-muscle-protein-synthesis-mps--the-soy-ceiling) (Minimal Stack)
**Ingredients:**
*   2 Vegan Protein Patties + 1/2 cup Chickpeas
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min [4](../../07-appendices/master-citations.md#4-sulforaphane--inflammatory-control))
*   1/2 cup quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **No nigella. No clove. No rosemary.**

**Instructions:**
1.  Sear patties with spice mix.
2.  Sear 5-6 min/side (165°F).
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.

## Salad: Bitter Reset
**Ingredients:**
*   2 cups arugula
*   2 tbsp sauerkraut
*   1 tbsp sesame
*   **1/2 cup microgreens**
*   **Generous handful fresh cilantro**
*   2 tbsp green onions
*   Dressing: Olive oil + Balsamic + 1 tbsp Tomato Puree + 1 tsp Dijon Mustard + Pinch Turmeric + Pinch Black Pepper

**Note:** No pomegranate. No walnuts. No pumpkin.
