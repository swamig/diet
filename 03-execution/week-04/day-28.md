## Main Meal: Vegan Patties [8](../../07-appendices/master-citations.md#8-protein-architecture-muscle-protein-synthesis-mps--the-soy-ceiling) (Minimal Stack)
**Ingredients:**
*   2 Vegan Protein Patties + 1/2 cup Chickpeas
*   45g cooked quinoa (1/4 cup)
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper

**Instructions:**
1.  Sear patties with spice mix.
2.  Sear 5-6 min/side (165°F).
