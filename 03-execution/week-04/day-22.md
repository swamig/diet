## Hydration
**Morning: Ajwain Water**
*   1/4 tsp ajwain
*   Simmer 2 min, steep 10 min.

**Night: Tulsi Infusion**
*   1 tsp dried tulsi
*   Warm water (pH 8+ Optional)
*   Steep 10 mins. Drink warm 60 min before sleep.

## Main Meal: Vegan Patties [8](../../07-appendices/master-citations.md#8-protein-architecture-muscle-protein-synthesis-mps--the-soy-ceiling) (Low Oxalate Emphasis)
**Ingredients:**
*   2 Vegan Protein Patties + 1/2 cup Chickpeas
*   1/2 cup quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **No nigella. No clove.**

**Instructions:**
1.  Sear patties with spice mix.
2.  Sear 5-6 min/side (165°F).
