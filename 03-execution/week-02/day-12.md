## Objective
Moderate antimicrobial (Clove Day).

## Timing
*   **06:30** – Fasted cardio
*   **07:15** – Clove infusion
*   **12:00** – Break fast
*   **14:30** – Salad
*   **16:30** – Resistance
*   **17:30** – Protein
*   **17:50** – Recovery
*   **21:00** – Mineral water

## Hydration
**Morning: Clove Infusion**
*   1 whole clove
*   Simmer 2 min, steep 8 min. Remove clove.

**Night: Mineral Water**
*   Warm water + pinch sea salt.

## Main Meal: Tofu (Low Polyphenol Day)
**Ingredients:**
*   200g firm tofu (pressed 15 min)
*   150g broccoli (rested 30 min)
*   1/2 cup quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **No nigella.**

**Instructions:**
1.  Rub tofu with spice mix.
2.  Sear 3-4 min/side.
3.  Sauté broccoli 3-4 min, add mustard powder off heat.

## Salad
**Ingredients:**
*   2 cups arugula
*   2 tbsp sauerkraut
*   1 tbsp walnuts
*   **1/2 cup microgreens**
*   **Generous handful fresh cilantro**
*   2 tbsp green onions
*   Dressing: Olive oil + Balsamic + 1 tbsp Tomato Puree + 1 tsp Dijon Mustard + Pinch Turmeric + Pinch Black Pepper

**Note:** No pomegranate today.

## Post-Workout Protocol

- Standard 17:30 whey isolate + creatine window unless this day explicitly changes it.

## Recovery Bowl
*   Greek yogurt
*   Casein
*   Blueberries
*   **No cacao (avoid stacking clove + cacao).**

## Clinical Rationale
*   Clove provides potent antimicrobial modulation.
*   Spacing ensures no other heavy polyphenol/antimicrobial load.
