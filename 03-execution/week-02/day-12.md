## Hydration
**Morning: Clove Infusion**
*   1 whole clove
*   Simmer 2 min, steep 8 min. Remove clove.

**Night: Mineral Water**
*   Warm water + pinch sea salt.

## Main Meal: Tofu (High Capsaicin Pulse)
**Ingredients:**
*   200g firm tofu (pressed 15 min)
*   1/2 cup quinoa
*   1 tsp olive oil
*   1/2 tsp turmeric
*   1/2 tsp cumin
*   1/2 tsp coriander powder
*   1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
*   1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **No nigella.**

**Instructions:**
1.  Rub tofu with spice mix.
2.  Sear 3-4 min/side.

## Salad
