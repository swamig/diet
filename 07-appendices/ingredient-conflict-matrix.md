# Ingredient Conflict Matrix

[Back to Appendices](README.md) | [Back to Manual](../README.md)

This matrix captures the main protocol-level conflicts, spacing rules, and "do not stack blindly" pairs that show up across the manual.

| Pair or Situation | Why It Matters in This Protocol | What to Do | Reference |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Turmeric without black pepper** | Curcumin absorption is treated as incomplete without piperine support. | Keep the pair together as the default daily anchor. | [Inflammatory Control](../01-foundations/02-inflammatory-control.md), [Turmeric](../02-ingredients/turmeric.md), [Black Pepper](../02-ingredients/black-pepper.md) |
| **Chocolate whey + cacao nibs on the same day** | The protocol treats this as a cumulative oxalate load. | Skip cacao nibs on chocolate whey days. | [Supplement Cycling](../04-advanced-control/supplement-cycling.md) |
| **Mud Water + the normal turmeric / cacao pattern** | Mud Water already adds turmeric and cacao pressure into a system that is spacing both deliberately. | Limit it to 1-2x weekly and do not pair it with a normal cacao or turmeric-heavy day. | [Supplement Cycling](../04-advanced-control/supplement-cycling.md) |
| **Moringa + clove or high-dose ajwain on the same day** | Too many antimicrobial or detox-leaning inputs can distort the intended hormesis baseline. | Keep moringa on separate days from the heavier antimicrobial stack. | [Supplement Cycling](../04-advanced-control/supplement-cycling.md), [Antimicrobial Stacking Framework](../04-advanced-control/antimicrobial-stacking.md) |
| **Broccoli cooked immediately after chopping** | Heat can erase the sulforaphane workflow if the myrosinase reaction has not finished. | Chop first, rest 30 minutes, then cook briefly and add mustard powder off heat. | [Inflammatory Control](../01-foundations/02-inflammatory-control.md), [Sunday Prep](../05-practical/sunday-prep.md) |
| **Cook-and-chill starches reheated aggressively** | High heat works against the resistant starch structure the prep system is trying to preserve. | Reheat gently and keep warm components separate from cold ones. | [Resistant Starch Cycling](../01-foundations/05-resistant-starch.md), [Sunday Prep](../05-practical/sunday-prep.md), [Container System](../05-practical/container-system.md) |
| **Pre-ground black pepper inside batch spice jars** | The manual treats fresh-ground pepper as the potency-preserving move. | Keep pepper out of the batch jars and grind it fresh at cooking time. | [Container System](../05-practical/container-system.md) |
| **Flavored whey outside the post-workout window** | Sweeteners and chocolate flavors are treated as manageable only when tightly timed. | Keep flavored whey strictly at 17:30 and phase toward unflavored whey. | [Supplement Cycling](../04-advanced-control/supplement-cycling.md) |
| **Cacao without the planned dairy recovery context** | The protocol uses Greek yogurt and calcium as part of the oxalate-management logic. | Keep cacao inside the recovery bowl structure rather than as a free-floating add-on. | [Container System](../05-practical/container-system.md), [Oxalate Management](../04-advanced-control/oxalate-management.md) |

## How to Use This Page

- Check this before making substitutions or combining optional extras.
- Use it as a quick operator reference, then jump into the linked source page if a conflict keeps recurring.
- If a rule appears here and also in a day page, the day page wins for that day.
