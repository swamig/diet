# Biochemical Dosing & Macro Calculators

[Back to Appendices](README.md) | [Back to Manual](../README.md)

This protocol scales based on body mass and metabolic output. Use these formulas to tune the exact dosages of calories, protein, and pharmacological signals (like Sulforaphane) to your specific biology.

---

## 1. The Energy Expenditure Calculator (BMR & TDEE)
To maintain a precise fat-loss deficit while preserving lean muscle mass, you must calculate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).

**Step 1: Calculate Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)**
Using the clinically validated Mifflin-St Jeor Equation:
*   **Men:** `(10 × weight in kg) + (6.25 × height in cm) - (5 × age in years) + 5`
*   **Women:** `(10 × weight in kg) + (6.25 × height in cm) - (5 × age in years) - 161`

**Step 2: Calculate TDEE (Activity Multiplier)**
Multiply your BMR by the protocol's metabolic demand:
*   **Cardio-Only Rest Days:** `BMR × 1.375` (Light activity)
*   **Heavy Lift / VO2 Max Days:** `BMR × 1.55` (Moderate to high activity)

**Step 3: The Protocol Deficit**
Subtract **400 to 500 kcal** from your TDEE to find your daily protocol target. *(Example: A 5'7", 185lb, 42-year-old man has a TDEE of ~2,400 kcal on lift days. Protocol target = 1,900 kcal).*

---

## 2. The Protein Preservation Calculator
To prevent the body from burning muscle tissue in a caloric deficit, protein intake must be artificially high.

*   **The Math:** `2.0g to 2.4g of Protein per kg of Body Weight`
*   *Example for 84kg (185 lbs):* 84 × 2.2 = **~185g of Protein Maximum.**
*   *Protocol Baseline:* The protocol defaults to **140g - 160g** daily, which fulfills the 1.8g/kg clinical minimum for lean mass preservation without overtaxing the urea cycle.

---

## 3. The Sulforaphane (Nrf2) Dosing Calculator
Sulforaphane activates the Nrf2 liver detoxification pathway on a hormetic curve. Overdosing spills over the receptors; underdosing fails to trigger DNA repair.

**Step 1: Find your Clinical Target**
*   **Therapeutic Dose:** `0.2mg to 0.4mg of Sulforaphane per kg of Body Weight`
*   *Example for 84kg (185 lbs):* 84 × 0.3mg = **~25mg of Sulforaphane.**

**Step 2: Convert to Microgreens/Sprouts**
*   **Raw Broccoli Sprouts (3-day water-germinated):** Yield ~73mg per 1 oz (28g).
*   **Raw Broccoli Microgreens (10-day soil-grown):** Yield ~35mg per 1 oz (28g).

**The Daily Standard Translation:**
*   **1/4 Cup (approx. 15g-20g):** Yields ~25mg - 35mg. Perfect for the baseline clinical requirement (Rest Days).
*   **1/2 Cup (approx. 30g-40g):** Yields ~50mg - 70mg. Perfect for the performance buffer required to clear oxidative stress from heavy lifting or 4x4s.

---

## 4. The Omega-3 / Lipid Calculator
The protocol relies on a strict Omega-3 to Omega-6 ratio to lower systemic inflammation.

*   **Daily Minimum ALA (Alpha-Linolenic Acid):** 1.6g for men, 1.1g for women.
*   **Protocol Fulfillment:** 1 tbsp of Chia seeds (2.5g ALA), 1 tbsp of Flaxseeds (2.4g ALA), or 1 tbsp of Walnuts (2.5g ALA) easily maxes out this daily clinical requirement. This is why the "1-1-1" daily seed rotation is non-negotiable.
