# Spice Conversions & Internal Temperatures

## Spice Conversions

| Spice | 1 tsp (grams) | 1 tbsp (grams) | 1/2 tsp (grams) | 1/4 tsp (grams) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Turmeric (Ground)** | 3.0g | 9.0g | 1.5g | 0.75g |
| **Cumin (Seeds)** | 2.5g | 7.5g | 1.25g | 0.6g |
| **Black Pepper (Ground)** | 2.5g | 7.5g | 1.25g | 0.6g |
| **Black Jeera (Nigella)** | 2.7g | 8.0g | 1.35g | 0.7g |
| **Ajwain (Seeds)** | 2.2g | 6.5g | 1.1g | 0.5g |
| **Clove (Whole)** | ~1g/clove | - | - | - |
| **Saffron (Threads)** | ~0.05g | - | - | - |
| **Cinnamon (Ground)** | 2.6g | 7.8g | 1.3g | 0.65g |

## Internal Temperatures (Proteins)

| Protein | Target Temp (Internal) | Rest Time | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vegan Patties** | 165°F (74°C) internal (if steaming thick patties) | 10-15 min (Steam) | Sear after steaming for crust. |
| **Tofu** | Until seared | - | Texture preference. |
| **Lentils** | Tender | - | Simmer until soft. |

## Oil Smoke Points

| Oil | Smoke Point | Best Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Extra Virgin Olive Oil** | 375°F (190°C) | Sauté, Finishing, Low Heat |
| **Avocado Oil** | 520°F (270°C) | High Heat Sear, Grilling |
| **Mustard Oil** | 480°F (250°C) | Tempering, High Heat |
| **Ghee** | 485°F (250°C) | Tempering, Sauté |
